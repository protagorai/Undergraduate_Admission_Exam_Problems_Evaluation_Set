\section*{Problem 16}
Dat je skup $S = \left\{ \left(\frac{-1+i\sqrt{3}}{2}\right)^{2019}, \text{Im}\left(\left(\frac{1+i}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^{2019}\right), 0.3333333, \frac{\pi}{3}, \sin\frac{\pi}{3}, \frac{22}{7} \right\}$ i skup racionalnih brojeva $\mathbb{Q} = \left\{ \frac{p}{q} \mid p\in\mathbb{Z}, q\in\mathbb{N} \right\}, i^2=-1$. Broj elemenata skupa $S \cap \mathbb{Q}$ jeste:

\subsection*{Solution}
Analizirajmo elemente skupa $S$:
1. $z_1 = \frac{-1+i\sqrt{3}}{2} = e^{i\frac{2\pi}{3}}$.
$z_1^{2019} = e^{i\frac{2\pi}{3} \cdot 2019} = e^{i 2\pi \cdot 673} = 1 \in \mathbb{Q}$.
2. $z_2 = \frac{1+i}{\sqrt{2}} = e^{i\frac{\pi}{4}}$.
$z_2^{2019} = e^{i\frac{2019\pi}{4}}$.
$2019 = 4 \cdot 504 + 3$, pa je $2019\pi/4 \equiv 3\pi/4 \pmod{2\pi}$.
$\text{Im}(z_2^{2019}) = \sin(3\pi/4) = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \notin \mathbb{Q}$.
3. $0.3333333 = \frac{3333333}{10000000} \in \mathbb{Q}$.
4. $\frac{\pi}{3} \notin \mathbb{Q}$.
5. $\sin\frac{\pi}{3} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \notin \mathbb{Q}$.
6. $\frac{22}{7} \in \mathbb{Q}$.

Racionalni elementi su: $1$, $0.3333333$, $\frac{22}{7}$.
Ukupno ih je 3.

\subsection*{Answer}
$3$ (Opcija \textbf{B})
