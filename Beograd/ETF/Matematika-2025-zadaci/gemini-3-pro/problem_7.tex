\section*{Problem 7}
Broj realnih re≈°enja sistema $x^2 - 3xy + 5y^2 = 1$, $x^2 + xy + y^2 = 1$ je:

\subsection*{Solution}

Subtract the second equation from the first:
\[
(x^2 - 3xy + 5y^2) - (x^2 + xy + y^2) = 1 - 1
\]
\[
-4xy + 4y^2 = 0 \implies -4y(x - y) = 0.
\]
Case 1: $y = 0$.
Substitute into the second equation:
\[
x^2 + 0 + 0 = 1 \implies x^2 = 1 \implies x = \pm 1.
\]
Solutions: $(1, 0)$ and $(-1, 0)$.

Case 2: $x - y = 0 \implies x = y$.
Substitute into the second equation:
\[
x^2 + x(x) + x^2 = 1 \implies 3x^2 = 1 \implies x^2 = \frac{1}{3} \implies x = \pm \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}.
\]
Since $y=x$, solutions are $(\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}, \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}})$ and $(-\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}, -\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}})$.

All 4 solutions are real and distinct.


\subsection*{Answer}
4 (option \textbf{A}).
