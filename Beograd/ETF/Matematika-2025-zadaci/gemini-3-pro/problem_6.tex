\section*{Problem 6}
Ako je duži krak pravouglog trapeza jednak 10, a poluprečnik kružnice upisane u taj trapez jednak 4, onda je površina tog trapeza jednaka:

\subsection*{Solution}

Let the trapezoid be $ABCD$ with right angles at $A$ and $D$.
The height of the trapezoid is $h = 2r = 2(4) = 8$.
Since it is a right trapezoid, the side perpendicular to the bases is $AD = h = 8$.
The longer leg (slanted side) is $BC = c = 10$.
Since the circle is inscribed, the trapezoid is a tangential quadrilateral.
The property of a tangential quadrilateral is that the sums of opposite sides are equal:
\[
a + b = h + c,
\]
where $a$ and $b$ are the lengths of the parallel bases.
\[
a + b = 8 + 10 = 18.
\]
The area of the trapezoid is:
\[
P = \frac{a+b}{2} \cdot h = \frac{18}{2} \cdot 8 = 9 \cdot 8 = 72.
\]


\subsection*{Answer}
72 (option \textbf{E}).
