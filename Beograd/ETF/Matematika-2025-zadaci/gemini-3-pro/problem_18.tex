\section*{Problem 18}
Skup svih realnih rešenja nejednačine $(2\sin x)^{\sin x - \sin 2x} \ge 1$ na intervalu $(0, \pi)$ je:

\subsection*{Solution}

Base $2\sin x > 0$.
1) $2\sin x = 1 \implies \sin x = 1/2 \implies x = \pi/6, 5\pi/6$. Solution.
2) $2\sin x > 1 \implies x \in (\pi/6, 5\pi/6)$.
Exponent $\ge 0$: $\sin x (1-2\cos x) \ge 0$.
Since $\sin x > 0$, need $\cos x \le 1/2$.
In $(\pi/6, 5\pi/6)$, $\cos x \le 1/2$ for $x \in [\pi/3, 5\pi/6)$.
Intersection: $[\pi/3, 5\pi/6)$.
3) $2\sin x < 1 \implies x \in (0, \pi/6) \cup (5\pi/6, \pi)$.
Exponent $\le 0$: $1-2\cos x \le 0 \implies \cos x \ge 1/2$.
In $(0, \pi/6)$, $\cos x > \sqrt{3}/2 > 1/2$. True.
In $(5\pi/6, \pi)$, $\cos x < 0$. False.
Intersection: $(0, \pi/6)$.
Union: $(0, \pi/6] \cup [\pi/3, 5\pi/6]$.


\subsection*{Answer}
(0, \frac{\pi}{6}] \cup [\frac{\pi}{3}, \frac{5\pi}{6}] (option \textbf{B}).
