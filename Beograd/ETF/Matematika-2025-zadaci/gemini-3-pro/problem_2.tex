\section*{Problem 2}
Ako je povr≈°ina lopte jednaka $S$ cm$^2$, a njena zapremina jednaka $S$ cm$^3$, onda je $S$ jednako:

\subsection*{Solution}

Let $r$ be the radius of the sphere.
The surface area is $P = 4\pi r^2 = S$.
The volume is $V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 = S$.
Equating the numerical values of area and volume (ignoring units):
\[
4\pi r^2 = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3.
\]
Since $r \neq 0$, we can divide by $4\pi r^2$:
\[
1 = \frac{1}{3}r \implies r = 3.
\]
Now we calculate $S$:
\[
S = 4\pi (3)^2 = 36\pi.
\]


\subsection*{Answer}
36\pi (option \textbf{B}).
