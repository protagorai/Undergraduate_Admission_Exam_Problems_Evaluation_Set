\section*{Problem 8}
Zbir svih realnih rešenja jednačine $8^x - \frac{21}{4} \cdot 4^x + \frac{21}{4} \cdot 2^x - 1 = 0$ iznosi:

\subsection*{Solution}

Let $t = 2^x$. Then $8^x = t^3$ and $4^x = t^2$.
The equation becomes:
\[
t^3 - \frac{21}{4}t^2 + \frac{21}{4}t - 1 = 0.
\]
Multiply by 4 to clear fractions:
\[
4t^3 - 21t^2 + 21t - 4 = 0.
\]
We observe that $t=1$ is a solution: $4 - 21 + 21 - 4 = 0$.
Perform polynomial division by $(t-1)$:
\[
4t^3 - 4t^2 - 17t^2 + 17t + 4t - 4 = 0
\]
\[
4t^2(t-1) - 17t(t-1) + 4(t-1) = 0
\]
\[
(t-1)(4t^2 - 17t + 4) = 0.
\]
Solve $4t^2 - 17t + 4 = 0$:
\[
t = \frac{17 \pm \sqrt{289 - 64}}{8} = \frac{17 \pm 15}{8}.
\]
$t_2 = \frac{32}{8} = 4$, $t_3 = \frac{2}{8} = \frac{1}{4}$.
The values for $t$ are $1, 4, \frac{1}{4}$.
Since $t = 2^x$:
1) $2^x = 1 \implies x = 0$.
2) $2^x = 4 \implies x = 2$.
3) $2^x = \frac{1}{4} \implies x = -2$.
The sum of real solutions is $0 + 2 + (-2) = 0$.


\subsection*{Answer}
0 (option \textbf{B}).
