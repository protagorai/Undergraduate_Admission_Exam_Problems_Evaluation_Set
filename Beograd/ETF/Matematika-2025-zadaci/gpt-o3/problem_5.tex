\section*{Problem 5}
Ako je $\sin\dfrac{\alpha}{2}=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt5}$ za $\alpha\in(\pi,2\pi)$, odrediti $\tan\alpha$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Za $\alpha\in(\pi,2\pi)$ imamo $\alpha/2\in(\pi/2,\pi)$, pa je $\cos(\alpha/2)<0$. Iz Pitagorine veze:
\[
\cos\frac{\alpha}{2}=-\sqrt{1-\sin^2\frac{\alpha}{2}}=-\sqrt{1-\frac15}=-\frac{2}{\sqrt5}.
\]
Tada je
\[
\tan\frac{\alpha}{2}=\frac{\sin(\alpha/2)}{\cos(\alpha/2)}=\frac{1/\sqrt5}{-2/\sqrt5}=-\frac12.
\]
Formula dvostrukog ugla za tangens:
\[
\tan\alpha=\frac{2\tan(\alpha/2)}{1-\tan^2(\alpha/2)}=\frac{2(-1/2)}{1-1/4}=\frac{-1}{3/4}=-\frac{4}{3}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\dfrac{4}{3}$ (option \textbf{B}).
