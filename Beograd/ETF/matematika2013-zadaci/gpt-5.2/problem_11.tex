\section*{Zadatak 11}
Neka su \(x_1\) i \(x_2\) koreni jednačine \(x^2+bx+c=0\) (\(b,c\in\mathbb{R}\setminus\{0\}\)). Izračunati \(x_1^4+x_2^4\).

\subsection*{Rešenje}
Po Vijetovim formulama:
\[
x_1+x_2=-b,\qquad x_1x_2=c.
\]
Računamo:
\[
x_1^2+x_2^2=(x_1+x_2)^2-2x_1x_2=b^2-2c.
\]
Zatim
\[
x_1^4+x_2^4=(x_1^2+x_2^2)^2-2(x_1x_2)^2=(b^2-2c)^2-2c^2.
\]
\[
=(b^4-4b^2c+4c^2)-2c^2=b^4-4b^2c+2c^2.
\]

\subsection*{Odgovor}
\[
\boxed{(A)\ b^4-4b^2c+2c^2}
\]
