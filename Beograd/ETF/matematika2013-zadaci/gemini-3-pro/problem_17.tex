\section*{Problem 17}
Zbir prva tri člana rastuće aritmetičke progresije je 54. Ako od prvog člana te progresije oduzmemo 3, drugi član ostane nepromenjen, a trećem članu dodamo 12, dobijamo prva tri člana geometrijske progresije. Količnik te geometrijske progresije je:
\[ (A)\ 6 \quad (B)\ 2 \quad (C)\ -3 \quad (D)\ \frac{1}{2} \quad (E)\ \frac{1}{6} \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the arithmetic progression (AP) terms be $a-d, a, a+d$.
Sum: $(a-d) + a + (a+d) = 3a = 54 \implies a = 18$.
The AP terms are $18-d, 18, 18+d$. Since it is increasing, $d > 0$.
The geometric progression (GP) terms are obtained by:
1st term: $(18-d) - 3 = 15-d$
2nd term: $18$
3rd term: $(18+d) + 12 = 30+d$
For GP, $b^2 = ac$:
\[ 18^2 = (15-d)(30+d) \]
\[ 324 = 450 + 15d - 30d - d^2 \]
\[ 324 = 450 - 15d - d^2 \]
\[ d^2 + 15d - 126 = 0 \]
Solve for $d$:
\[ d = \frac{-15 \pm \sqrt{225 - 4(-126)}}{2} = \frac{-15 \pm \sqrt{225 + 504}}{2} = \frac{-15 \pm \sqrt{729}}{2} \]
\[ d = \frac{-15 \pm 27}{2} \]
Possible values: $d_1 = \frac{12}{2} = 6$ and $d_2 = \frac{-42}{2} = -21$.
Since AP is increasing, $d > 0$, so $d=6$.
The GP terms are:
1st: $15-6 = 9$
2nd: $18$
3rd: $30+6 = 36$
The common ratio $q = \frac{18}{9} = 2$ (also $\frac{36}{18} = 2$).

\subsection*{Answer}
$2$ (option \textbf{B}).
