\section*{Problem 3}
Vrednost izraza $\frac{x^{0,5}+1}{x+x^{0,5}+1} : \frac{1}{x^{1,5}-1}$, za $x \ge 0, x \ne 1$ je:
\[ (A)\ x^2-1 \quad (B)\ 2x-1 \quad (C)\ 2\sqrt{x}-1 \quad (D)\ x-1 \quad (E)\ \sqrt{x}-1 \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's simplify the expression. Note that $x^{0,5} = \sqrt{x}$ and $x^{1,5} = (\sqrt{x})^3 = \sqrt{x}^3$.
Let $t = \sqrt{x}$. The expression becomes:
\[ \frac{t+1}{t^2+t+1} : \frac{1}{t^3-1} \]
We know that $t^3 - 1 = (t-1)(t^2+t+1)$.
So the expression is:
\[ \frac{t+1}{t^2+t+1} \cdot \frac{t^3-1}{1} \]
\[ = \frac{t+1}{t^2+t+1} \cdot (t-1)(t^2+t+1) \]
Cancel $t^2+t+1$:
\[ = (t+1)(t-1) \]
\[ = t^2 - 1 \]
Substitute back $t = \sqrt{x}$:
\[ = (\sqrt{x})^2 - 1 = x - 1 \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$x - 1$ (option \textbf{D}).
