\section*{Problem 14}
U valjak prečnika osnove $14\sqrt{3}$ cm i visine $20$ cm upisana je prava trostrana prizma čija osnova je trougao $ABC$ čija je stranica $BC=9$ cm, a ugao naspram stranice $AC$ je $120^\circ$. Zapremina prizme (u cm$^3$) je:
\[ (A)\ 1890\sqrt{3} \quad (B)\ 3780\sqrt{3} \quad (C)\ 810\sqrt{3} \quad (D)\ 675\sqrt{3} \quad (E)\ 825\sqrt{3} \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
Cylinder diameter $D = 2R = 14\sqrt{3} \implies R = 7\sqrt{3}$.
Height $H = 20$.
The prism is inscribed, so the base triangle $ABC$ is inscribed in the base circle of radius $R$.
Given: side $a = BC = 9$.
Angle opposite to side $b=AC$ is $\beta = \angle ABC = 120^\circ$.
Let sides be $a, b, c$ and angles $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$.
Using the extended Sine Law:
\[ \frac{b}{\sin\beta} = 2R \]
\[ \frac{b}{\sin 120^\circ} = 14\sqrt{3} \]
\[ b = 14\sqrt{3} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = 7 \cdot 3 = 21 \]
So $AC = 21$.
Now we have sides $a=9$, $b=21$ and angle $\beta=120^\circ$ between $c$ and $a$. Wait, $\beta$ is opposite to $b$.
Using Law of Cosines on side $b$:
\[ b^2 = a^2 + c^2 - 2ac \cos\beta \]
\[ 21^2 = 9^2 + c^2 - 2(9)c \cos 120^\circ \]
\[ 441 = 81 + c^2 - 18c (-1/2) \]
\[ 441 = 81 + c^2 + 9c \]
\[ c^2 + 9c + 81 - 441 = 0 \]
\[ c^2 + 9c - 360 = 0 \]
Solving for $c$:
\[ c = \frac{-9 \pm \sqrt{81 - 4(1)(-360)}}{2} = \frac{-9 \pm \sqrt{81 + 1440}}{2} = \frac{-9 \pm \sqrt{1521}}{2} \]
\[ \sqrt{1521} = 39 \]
\[ c = \frac{-9 + 39}{2} = 15 \quad (\text{since } c>0) \]
So the sides are $a=9, b=21, c=15$.
Area of base triangle $ABC$:
\[ B = \frac{1}{2} ac \sin\beta = \frac{1}{2} \cdot 9 \cdot 15 \cdot \sin 120^\circ = \frac{135}{2} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = \frac{135\sqrt{3}}{4} \]
Volume of prism $V = B \cdot H$:
\[ V = \frac{135\sqrt{3}}{4} \cdot 20 = 135\sqrt{3} \cdot 5 = 675\sqrt{3} \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$675\sqrt{3}$ (option \textbf{D}).
