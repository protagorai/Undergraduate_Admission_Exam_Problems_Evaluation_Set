\section*{Problem 4}
Ako $30\%$ broja $2n$ iznosi $2013$, tada $40\%$ broja $5n$ ($n \in \mathbf{N}$), iznosi:
\[ (A)\ 6710 \quad (B)\ 3355 \quad (C)\ 1342 \quad (D)\ 6038 \quad (E)\ 2820 \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
First, find $n$ from the given condition:
\[ 30\% \cdot 2n = 2013 \]
\[ 0.3 \cdot 2n = 2013 \]
\[ 0.6n = 2013 \]
\[ n = \frac{2013}{0.6} = \frac{20130}{6} = 3355 \]
Now we need to find $40\%$ of $5n$:
\[ 0.4 \cdot 5n = 2n \]
\[ 2n = 2 \cdot 3355 = 6710 \]
Alternatively:
$0.4 \cdot 5n = 2n$. We know $0.6n = 2013 \implies 0.2n = 2013/3 = 671$.
$2n = 10 \cdot (0.2n) = 10 \cdot 671 = 6710$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$6710$ (option \textbf{A}).
