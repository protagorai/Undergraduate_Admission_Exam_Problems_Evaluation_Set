\section*{Problem 1}
Vrednost izraza $2x^2 - 2,4x - 1,7$ za $x = 7 \cdot 10^{-1}$ iznosi:
\[ (A)\ 1 \quad (B)\ -17,52 \quad (C)\ 6,42 \quad (D)\ -2,40 \quad (E)\ -2,89 \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
We are given the expression $2x^2 - 2,4x - 1,7$ and $x = 7 \cdot 10^{-1} = 0,7$.
Substitute $x = 0,7$ into the expression:
\[ 2(0,7)^2 - 2,4(0,7) - 1,7 \]
Calculate each term:
\[ (0,7)^2 = 0,49 \]
\[ 2 \cdot 0,49 = 0,98 \]
\[ 2,4 \cdot 0,7 = 1,68 \]
Now substitute back:
\[ 0,98 - 1,68 - 1,7 \]
\[ = -0,7 - 1,7 \]
\[ = -2,40 \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-2,40$ (option \textbf{D}).
