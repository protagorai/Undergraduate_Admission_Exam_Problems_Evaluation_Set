\section*{Problem 5}
U jednakokraki trougao čija je osnovica $a=10$ cm i krak $b=13$ cm upisan je kvadrat tako da mu dva temena leže na osnovici trougla, a druga dva na kracima. Dužina stranice kvadrata (u cm) jednaka je:
\[ (A)\ \frac{64}{11} \quad (B)\ \frac{63}{11} \quad (C)\ \frac{62}{11} \quad (D)\ \frac{61}{11} \quad (E)\ \frac{60}{11} \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam} \]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the base of the isosceles triangle be $AB = a = 10$ and the equal sides be $AC = BC = b = 13$.
Let $h$ be the altitude to the base.
\[ h = \sqrt{b^2 - (a/2)^2} = \sqrt{13^2 - 5^2} = \sqrt{169 - 25} = \sqrt{144} = 12 \text{ cm} \]
Let $x$ be the side length of the inscribed square.
The square cuts off a smaller triangle at the top which is similar to the original triangle.
The base of the smaller triangle lies on the top side of the square, so its length is $x$.
The altitude of the smaller triangle is $h - x$.
Using similarity of triangles:
\[ \frac{\text{base}_{\text{small}}}{\text{base}_{\text{large}}} = \frac{\text{height}_{\text{small}}}{\text{height}_{\text{large}}} \]
\[ \frac{x}{a} = \frac{h - x}{h} \]
Substitute the values:
\[ \frac{x}{10} = \frac{12 - x}{12} \]
\[ 12x = 10(12 - x) \]
\[ 12x = 120 - 10x \]
\[ 22x = 120 \]
\[ x = \frac{120}{22} = \frac{60}{11} \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{60}{11}$ (option \textbf{E}).
