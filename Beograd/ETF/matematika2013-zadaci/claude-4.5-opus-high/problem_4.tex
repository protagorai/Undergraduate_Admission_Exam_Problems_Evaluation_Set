\section*{Problem 4}
If 30\% of the number $2n$ is 2013, then 40\% of the number $5n$ (where $n \in \mathbf{N}$) equals:

Options: (A) 6710 \quad (B) 3355 \quad (C) 1342 \quad (D) 6038 \quad (E) 2820 \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
From the condition that 30\% of $2n$ equals 2013:
\[
0.30 \cdot 2n = 2013
\]
\[
0.6n = 2013
\]
\[
n = \frac{2013}{0.6} = 3355
\]

Now we need to find 40\% of $5n$:
\[
0.40 \cdot 5n = 2n = 2 \cdot 3355 = 6710
\]

Alternatively:
\[
0.40 \cdot 5 \cdot 3355 = 2 \cdot 3355 = 6710
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$6710$ (option \textbf{A}).
