\section*{Problem 1}
Vrednost izraza $2x^2 - 2,4x - 1,7$ za $x = 7 \cdot 10^{-1}$ iznosi:

\subsection*{Solution}
Prvo zamenimo $x = 7 \cdot 10^{-1} = 0.7$:

\begin{align}
2x^2 - 2.4x - 1.7 &= 2(0.7)^2 - 2.4(0.7) - 1.7\\
&= 2(0.49) - 1.68 - 1.7\\
&= 0.98 - 1.68 - 1.7\\
&= 0.98 - 3.38\\
&= -2.4
\end{align}

\subsection*{Answer}
$-2.40$ (option \textbf{D}).
