\section*{Zadatak 1}

Tra\v zi se vrednost izraza
\[
\log_{4}3\cdot \log_{5}4\cdot \log_{6}5\cdot \log_{7}6\cdot \log_{8}7\cdot \log_{9}8.
\]
Koristimo promenu osnove: \(\log_a b=\dfrac{\ln b}{\ln a}\). Tada
\[
\frac{\ln 3}{\ln 4}\cdot\frac{\ln 4}{\ln 5}\cdot\frac{\ln 5}{\ln 6}\cdot\frac{\ln 6}{\ln 7}\cdot\frac{\ln 7}{\ln 8}\cdot\frac{\ln 8}{\ln 9}
=\frac{\ln 3}{\ln 9}.
\]
Po\v sto je \(\ln 9=\ln(3^2)=2\ln 3\), sledi
\[
\frac{\ln 3}{\ln 9}=\frac{\ln 3}{2\ln 3}=\frac12.
\]

\subsection*{Odgovor}
\[
\boxed{\frac12}\qquad\text{(A)}
\]
