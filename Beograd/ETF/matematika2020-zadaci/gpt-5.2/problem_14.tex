\section*{Zadatak 14}

Re\v savamo nejedna\v cinu
\[
12(\arctan x)^2+\pi \arctan x-\pi^2\le 0.
\]
Uvedimo smenu \(t=\arctan x\). Tada je
\[
t\in\left(-\frac{\pi}{2},\frac{\pi}{2}\right),
\]
a nejedna\v cina postaje kvadratna:
\[
12t^2+\pi t-\pi^2\le 0.
\]
Nule polinoma su
\[
t=\frac{-\pi\pm \sqrt{\pi^2+48\pi^2}}{24}
=\frac{-\pi\pm 7\pi}{24}.
\]
Dakle
\[
t_1=\frac{-8\pi}{24}=-\frac{\pi}{3},\qquad
t_2=\frac{6\pi}{24}=\frac{\pi}{4}.
\]
Po\v sto je koeficijent uz \(t^2\) pozitivan, re\v senja su izme\dj u korena:
\[
t\in\left[-\frac{\pi}{3},\frac{\pi}{4}\right].
\]
Vra\'camo smenu:
\[
\arctan x\in\left[-\frac{\pi}{3},\frac{\pi}{4}\right]
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
x\in\left[\tan\!\left(-\frac{\pi}{3}\right),\tan\!\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)\right]
=[-\sqrt3,\,1].
\]
To je zatvoreni interval \([a,b]\).

\subsection*{Odgovor}
\[
\boxed{[a,b]}\qquad\text{(B)},\quad \text{gde je } a=-\sqrt3,\ b=1.
\]
