\section*{Problem 1}
Vrednost izraza $\log_4 3 \log_5 4 \log_6 5 \log_7 6 \log_8 7 \log_9 8$ jednaka je:
\[
(A)\ \frac{1}{2}\quad (B)\ \frac{1}{3}\quad (C)\ 2\quad (D)\ 3\quad (E)\ \frac{2}{3}\log_2 3\quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam}
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo formulu za promenu osnove logaritma $\log_a b = \frac{\ln b}{\ln a}$. Izraz postaje:
\[
\frac{\ln 3}{\ln 4} \cdot \frac{\ln 4}{\ln 5} \cdot \frac{\ln 5}{\ln 6} \cdot \frac{\ln 6}{\ln 7} \cdot \frac{\ln 7}{\ln 8} \cdot \frac{\ln 8}{\ln 9}
\]
Skraćivanjem dijagonalnih članova dobijamo:
\[
\frac{\ln 3}{\ln 9} = \log_9 3 = \log_{3^2} 3 = \frac{1}{2} \log_3 3 = \frac{1}{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{1}{2}$ (option \textbf{A}).
