\section*{Problem 4}
Izraz $5^{\frac{3-\log_{10}5}{\log_{10}25}}$ je jednak izrazu:

(A) $10\sqrt{2}$ \quad (B) $5$ \quad (C) $\displaystyle\frac{5}{\sqrt{2}}$ \quad (D) $\displaystyle\frac{10}{\sqrt{2}}$ \quad (E) $5^{\frac{1}{5}}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $\log_{10} 5 = a$. Then $\log_{10} 25 = \log_{10} 5^2 = 2a$.

The expression becomes:
\[
5^{\frac{3-a}{2a}}
\]

Since $\log_{10} 5 = \log_{10} \frac{10}{2} = 1 - \log_{10} 2$, we have $a = 1 - \log_{10} 2$.

But let's compute directly. Note that $5 = \frac{10}{2}$, so:
\[
\log_{10} 5 = 1 - \log_{10} 2
\]

Also, $\log_{10} 2 \approx 0.301$, so $\log_{10} 5 \approx 0.699$.

Let's use exact values. We have:
\[
5^{\frac{3-\log_{10}5}{2\log_{10}5}} = 5^{\frac{3}{2\log_{10}5} - \frac{1}{2}}
\]

Note that $\frac{1}{\log_{10}5} = \log_5 10$, so:
\[
\frac{3}{2\log_{10}5} = \frac{3}{2} \log_5 10
\]

Therefore:
\[
5^{\frac{3}{2}\log_5 10 - \frac{1}{2}} = 5^{\frac{3}{2}\log_5 10} \cdot 5^{-\frac{1}{2}} = 10^{\frac{3}{2}} \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} = \frac{10\sqrt{10}}{\sqrt{5}} = 10\sqrt{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$10\sqrt{2}$ (option \textbf{A}).
