\section*{Problem 1}
Vrednost izraza $2014^3 - 2013 \cdot 2014 \cdot 2015$ jednaka je:

(A) 1 \quad (B) 2013 \quad (C) 2014 \quad (D) 2015 \quad (E) $-1$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $n = 2014$. Then the expression becomes:
\[
n^3 - (n-1) \cdot n \cdot (n+1)
\]

Note that $(n-1) \cdot n \cdot (n+1) = n(n^2-1) = n^3 - n$.

Therefore:
\[
n^3 - (n^3 - n) = n^3 - n^3 + n = n = 2014
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$2014$ (option \textbf{C}).
