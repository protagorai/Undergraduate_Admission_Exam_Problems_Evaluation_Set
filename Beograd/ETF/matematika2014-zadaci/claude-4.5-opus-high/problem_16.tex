\section*{Problem 16}
Bočne strane trostrane piramide su pravougli trouglovi sa temenom pravog ugla u vrhu piramide. Površine tih bočnih strana su 6 cm$^2$, 8 cm$^2$ i 12 cm$^2$. Zapremina piramide je:

(A) $6$ cm$^3$ \quad (B) $8\sqrt{2}$ cm$^3$ \quad (C) $8$ cm$^3$ \quad (D) $6\sqrt{2}$ cm$^3$ \quad (E) $12$ cm$^3$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the apex of the pyramid be $V$ and the three edges from $V$ be $a$, $b$, $c$ (perpendicular to each other).

The three lateral faces are right triangles with legs being pairs of these edges:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Face with legs $a, b$: Area $= \frac{1}{2}ab$
    \item Face with legs $b, c$: Area $= \frac{1}{2}bc$
    \item Face with legs $a, c$: Area $= \frac{1}{2}ac$
\end{itemize}

So we have:
\[
\frac{1}{2}ab = 6 \implies ab = 12
\]
\[
\frac{1}{2}bc = 8 \implies bc = 16
\]
\[
\frac{1}{2}ac = 12 \implies ac = 24
\]

Multiplying all three:
\[
(ab)(bc)(ac) = 12 \cdot 16 \cdot 24 = a^2b^2c^2
\]
\[
a^2b^2c^2 = 4608
\]
\[
abc = \sqrt{4608} = \sqrt{4608} = \sqrt{1024 \cdot 4.5} = 48\sqrt{2}
\]

Let me verify: $4608 = 2^9 \cdot 9 = 512 \cdot 9$. Actually $12 \cdot 16 \cdot 24 = 192 \cdot 24 = 4608$.
$4608 = 2^9 \cdot 3^2$, so $\sqrt{4608} = 2^{4.5} \cdot 3 = 16\sqrt{2} \cdot 3 = 48\sqrt{2}$.

The volume of a pyramid with three mutually perpendicular edges $a, b, c$ from the apex is:
\[
V = \frac{1}{6}abc = \frac{1}{6} \cdot 48\sqrt{2} = 8\sqrt{2} \text{ cm}^3
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$8\sqrt{2}$ cm$^3$ (option \textbf{B}).
