\section*{Problem 8}
Odnos binomnih koeficijenata uz stepen $x^{1007}$, $(x \in (0, +\infty))$ u razvojima binoma $(1+x)^{2014}$ i $(1+x)^{2013}$ redom, iznosi:

(A) $\displaystyle\frac{1007}{1006}$ \quad (B) $2$ \quad (C) $\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}$ \quad (D) $\displaystyle\frac{1}{2014}$ \quad (E) $\displaystyle\frac{1}{2015}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
In the binomial expansion of $(1+x)^n$, the coefficient of $x^k$ is $\binom{n}{k}$.

For $(1+x)^{2014}$, the coefficient of $x^{1007}$ is:
\[
\binom{2014}{1007}
\]

For $(1+x)^{2013}$, the coefficient of $x^{1007}$ is:
\[
\binom{2013}{1007}
\]

The ratio is:
\[
\frac{\binom{2014}{1007}}{\binom{2013}{1007}} = \frac{\frac{2014!}{1007! \cdot 1007!}}{\frac{2013!}{1007! \cdot 1006!}}
\]

\[
= \frac{2014!}{1007! \cdot 1007!} \cdot \frac{1007! \cdot 1006!}{2013!}
\]

\[
= \frac{2014!}{2013!} \cdot \frac{1006!}{1007!}
\]

\[
= \frac{2014}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{1007} = \frac{2014}{1007} = 2
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$2$ (option \textbf{B}).
