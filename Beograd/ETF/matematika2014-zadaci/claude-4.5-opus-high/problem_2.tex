\section*{Problem 2}
Pojeftinjenje neke robe najpre za 10\%, a zatim za 20\%, jednako je pojeftinjenju iste robe za:

(A) 30\% \quad (B) 25\% \quad (C) 32\% \quad (D) 28\% \quad (E) 19\% \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the original price be $P$.

After a 10\% reduction, the price becomes:
\[
P_1 = P \cdot (1 - 0.10) = 0.90P
\]

After a further 20\% reduction, the price becomes:
\[
P_2 = P_1 \cdot (1 - 0.20) = 0.90P \cdot 0.80 = 0.72P
\]

The total reduction from the original price is:
\[
P - P_2 = P - 0.72P = 0.28P
\]

This represents a 28\% reduction.

\subsection*{Answer}
$28\%$ (option \textbf{D}).
