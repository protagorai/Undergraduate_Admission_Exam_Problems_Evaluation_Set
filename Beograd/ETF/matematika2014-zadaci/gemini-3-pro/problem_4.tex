\section*{Problem 4}
Izraz $5^{\frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{\log_{10} 25}}$ je jednak izrazu:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(A)] $10\sqrt{2}$
    \item[(B)] $5$
    \item[(C)] $\frac{5}{\sqrt{2}}$
    \item[(D)] $\frac{10}{\sqrt{2}}$
    \item[(E)] $5^{\frac{1}{5}}$
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $A$ be the expression. The exponent is:
\[
E = \frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{\log_{10} 25}
\]
Since $\log_{10} 25 = \log_{10} 5^2 = 2\log_{10} 5$:
\[
E = \frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{2\log_{10} 5} = \frac{3}{2\log_{10} 5} - \frac{1}{2}
\]
So the expression becomes:
\[
A = 5^{\frac{3}{2\log_{10} 5} - \frac{1}{2}} = \frac{5^{\frac{3}{2\log_{10} 5}}}{5^{1/2}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} \left(5^{\frac{1}{\log_{10} 5}}\right)^{3/2}
\]
Recall that $a^{\frac{1}{\log_b a}} = b$. Here $a=5, b=10$, so:
\[
5^{\frac{1}{\log_{10} 5}} = 10
\]
Thus:
\[
A = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} (10)^{3/2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}} 10\sqrt{10} = 10\sqrt{\frac{10}{5}} = 10\sqrt{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$10\sqrt{2}$ (option \textbf{(A)}).
