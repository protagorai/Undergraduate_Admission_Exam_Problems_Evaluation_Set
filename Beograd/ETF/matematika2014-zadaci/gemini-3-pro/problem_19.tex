\section*{Problem 19}
Od lista hartije kružnog oblika izrezan je kružni isečak od koga je napravljen konusni levak najveće zapremine. Centralni ugao tog kružnog isečka u radijanima je:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(A)] $\frac{\pi}{3}$
    \item[(B)] $\frac{2\pi}{3}\sqrt{6}$
    \item[(C)] $\frac{2\pi}{3}$
    \item[(D)] $\frac{2\pi}{\sqrt{3}}$
    \item[(E)] $\frac{\pi\sqrt{6}}{2}$
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $R$ be the radius of the circular sheet (which becomes the slant height of the cone).
Let $\theta$ be the central angle of the sector in radians.
The arc length of the sector is $L = R\theta$.
This arc becomes the circumference of the base of the cone. Let $r$ be the base radius of the cone.
\[
2\pi r = R\theta \implies r = \frac{R\theta}{2\pi}
\]
The height $h$ of the cone is given by Pythagorean theorem:
\[
h = \sqrt{R^2 - r^2} = \sqrt{R^2 - \left(\frac{R\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2} = R\sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2}
\]
The volume of the cone is:
\[
V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h = \frac{1}{3}\pi \left(\frac{R\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2 R\sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2}
\]
Let $x = \left(\frac{\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2$. Since $r < R$, $x \in (0, 1)$.
\[
V(x) = C \cdot x \sqrt{1-x} = C \sqrt{x^2(1-x)} = C \sqrt{x^2-x^3}
\]
where $C$ is a constant. To maximize $V$, we maximize $f(x) = x^2 - x^3$.
\[
f'(x) = 2x - 3x^2 = x(2-3x)
\]
Setting $f'(x) = 0$ gives $x = 2/3$.
So:
\[
\left(\frac{\theta}{2\pi}\right)^2 = \frac{2}{3} \implies \frac{\theta}{2\pi} = \sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} = \frac{\sqrt{6}}{3}
\]
\[
\theta = 2\pi \frac{\sqrt{6}}{3}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{2\pi}{3}\sqrt{6}$ (option \textbf{(B)}).
