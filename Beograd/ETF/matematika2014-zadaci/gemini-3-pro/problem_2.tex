\section*{Problem 2}
Pojeftinjenje neke robe najpre za $10\%$, a zatim za $20\%$, jednako je pojeftinjenju iste robe za:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(A)] $30\%$
    \item[(B)] $25\%$
    \item[(C)] $32\%$
    \item[(D)] $28\%$
    \item[(E)] $19\%$
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the initial price of the goods be $P$.
After the first reduction of $10\%$, the new price $P_1$ is:
\[
P_1 = P \left(1 - \frac{10}{100}\right) = 0.9P
\]
After the second reduction of $20\%$ on the new price $P_1$, the final price $P_2$ is:
\[
P_2 = P_1 \left(1 - \frac{20}{100}\right) = 0.9P \cdot 0.8 = 0.72P
\]
The total reduction is $P - P_2 = P - 0.72P = 0.28P$.
Expressed as a percentage, this is $28\%$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$28\%$ (option \textbf{(D)}).
