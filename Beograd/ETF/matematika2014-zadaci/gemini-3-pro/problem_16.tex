\section*{Problem 16}
Bočne strane trostrane piramide su pravougli trouglovi sa temenom pravog ugla u vrhu piramide. Površine tih bočnih strana su $6\text{ cm}^2$, $8\text{ cm}^2$ i $12\text{ cm}^2$. Zapremina piramide je:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[(A)] $6\text{ cm}^3$
    \item[(B)] $8\sqrt{2}\text{ cm}^3$
    \item[(C)] $8\text{ cm}^3$
    \item[(D)] $6\sqrt{2}\text{ cm}^3$
    \item[(E)] $12\text{ cm}^3$
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the vertex of the pyramid be $V$, and the base be $ABC$. The lateral faces $VAB, VBC, VCA$ are right triangles at $V$.
Let $VA=a, VB=b, VC=c$. The edges $a,b,c$ are mutually perpendicular.
The areas of the lateral faces are given by:
\[
S_1 = \text{Area}(VAB) = \frac{1}{2}ab = 6 \implies ab = 12
\]
\[
S_2 = \text{Area}(VBC) = \frac{1}{2}bc = 8 \implies bc = 16
\]
\[
S_3 = \text{Area}(VCA) = \frac{1}{2}ca = 12 \implies ca = 24
\]
Multiplying these equations:
\[
(ab)(bc)(ca) = 12 \cdot 16 \cdot 24
\]
\[
(abc)^2 = 12 \cdot 16 \cdot (2 \cdot 12) = 144 \cdot 32
\]
\[
abc = \sqrt{144 \cdot 16 \cdot 2} = 12 \cdot 4 \sqrt{2} = 48\sqrt{2}
\]
The volume of a tetrahedron with three mutually perpendicular edges $a,b,c$ meeting at a vertex is:
\[
V = \frac{1}{6}abc
\]
\[
V = \frac{1}{6} (48\sqrt{2}) = 8\sqrt{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$8\sqrt{2}\text{ cm}^3$ (option \textbf{(B)}).
