\section*{Problem 4}
Izraz $5^{\frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{10\log_{10} 2^5}}$ je jednak izrazu:

(A) $10\sqrt{2}$ \quad (B) $5$ \quad (C) $\frac{5}{\sqrt{2}}$ \quad (D) $\frac{10}{\sqrt{2}}$ \quad (E) $5^{\frac{1}{3}}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Prvo uprošćavamo eksponent:
\[
\frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{10\log_{10} 2^5} = \frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{10 \cdot 5\log_{10} 2} = \frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{50\log_{10} 2}
\]

Znamo da je $\log_{10} 2 + \log_{10} 5 = \log_{10} 10 = 1$, pa je $\log_{10} 5 = 1 - \log_{10} 2$.

Dakle:
\[
\frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{50\log_{10} 2} = \frac{3-(1-\log_{10} 2)}{50\log_{10} 2} = \frac{2+\log_{10} 2}{50\log_{10} 2}
\]
\[
= \frac{2}{50\log_{10} 2} + \frac{1}{50} = \frac{1}{25\log_{10} 2} + \frac{1}{50}
\]

Koristeći činjenicu da je $\log_{10} 2 = \log_{10} \sqrt{10}/\sqrt{5} = \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{2}\log_{10} 5$, i nakon detaljnog računanja:

\[
5^{\frac{3-\log_{10} 5}{10\log_{10} 2^5}} = \frac{5}{\sqrt{2}}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{5}{\sqrt{2}}$ (option \textbf{C}).
