\section*{Problem 14}
Dat je trougao $ABC$ sa stranicama $AB = \sqrt{2}$ cm i $AC = \sqrt{3}$ cm. Neka je tačka $D$ na stranici $BC$ tako da je $\angle BAD = 30°$ i $\angle CAD = 45°$. Dužina duži $AD$ iznosi (u cm):

(A) $\frac{\sqrt{6}}{2}$ \quad (B) $\frac{1}{\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{6}}$ \quad (C) $\sqrt{\frac{5}{2}}$ \quad (D) $\frac{\sqrt{6} + 1}{\sqrt{2} + \sqrt{6}}$ \quad (E) $\frac{1}{2}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Imamo trougao $ABC$ sa $AB = \sqrt{2}$, $AC = \sqrt{3}$, $\angle BAD = 30°$ i $\angle CAD = 45°$.

Dakle, $\angle BAC = \angle BAD + \angle CAD = 30° + 45° = 75°$.

Koristimo sinusovu teoremu u trouglovima $ABD$ i $ACD$:

U trouglu $ABD$:
\[
\frac{BD}{\sin 30°} = \frac{AD}{\sin \angle ABD} = \frac{AB}{\sin \angle ADB}
\]

U trouglu $ACD$:
\[
\frac{CD}{\sin 45°} = \frac{AD}{\sin \angle ACD} = \frac{AC}{\sin \angle ADC}
\]

Pošto je $\angle ADB + \angle ADC = 180°$, imamo $\sin \angle ADB = \sin \angle ADC$.

Iz sinusove teoreme:
\[
\frac{BD}{\sin 30°} = \frac{AB}{\sin \angle ADB} \Rightarrow BD = \frac{AB \sin 30°}{\sin \angle ADB} = \frac{\sqrt{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2}}{\sin \angle ADB}
\]

\[
\frac{CD}{\sin 45°} = \frac{AC}{\sin \angle ADC} \Rightarrow CD = \frac{AC \sin 45°}{\sin \angle ADC} = \frac{\sqrt{3} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}{\sin \angle ADB}
\]

Takođe, iz sinusove teoreme u trouglovima $ABD$ i $ACD$:
\[
\frac{AD}{\sin \angle ABD} = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sin \angle ADB}, \quad \frac{AD}{\sin \angle ACD} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{\sin \angle ADB}
\]

Koristeći formulu za površinu:
\[
S_{ABD} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot AB \cdot AD \cdot \sin 30° = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{2} \cdot AD \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{2} \cdot AD}{4}
\]

\[
S_{ACD} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot AC \cdot AD \cdot \sin 45° = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \sqrt{3} \cdot AD \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{6} \cdot AD}{4}
\]

Koristeći kosinusovu teoremu i dodatne kalkulacije, dobijamo:
\[
AD = \frac{\sqrt{6}}{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{\sqrt{6}}{2}$ (option \textbf{A}).
