\section*{Problem 15}
The set of all real values of $x$ for which the inequality $|4^{3x} - 2^{4+2x} \cdot 3^{x+1} + 20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x| \geq 8 \cdot 6^x (8^{x-1} + 6^x)$ holds (for some real numbers $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ such that $-\infty < a < b < c < d < +\infty$) is:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's simplify the given inequality step by step.

First, let's rewrite all terms using common bases. Let $y = 2^x$ and $z = 3^x$, so $6^x = 2^x \cdot 3^x = yz$ and $12^x = (4 \cdot 3)^x = 4^x \cdot 3^x = (2^2)^x \cdot 3^x = 2^{2x} \cdot 3^x = y^2z$.

The left side becomes:
\[|4^{3x} - 2^{4+2x} \cdot 3^{x+1} + 20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x|\]
\[= |(2^2)^{3x} - 2^4 \cdot 2^{2x} \cdot 3 \cdot 3^x + 20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x|\]
\[= |2^{6x} - 16 \cdot 2^{2x} \cdot 3 \cdot 3^x + 20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x|\]
\[= |y^6 - 48y^2z + 20y^2z^2|\]
\[= |y^6 - 48y^2z + 20y^2z^2|\]
\[= |y^2(y^4 - 48z + 20z^2)|\]
\[= y^2|y^4 - 48z + 20z^2|\]

The right side becomes:
\[8 \cdot 6^x (8^{x-1} + 6^x) = 8yz \left(\frac{8^x}{8} + 6^x\right) = 8yz \left(\frac{(2^3)^x}{8} + (yz)\right)\]
\[= 8yz \left(\frac{2^{3x}}{8} + yz\right) = 8yz \left(\frac{y^3}{8} + yz\right)\]
\[= 8yz \cdot \frac{y^3 + 8yz}{8} = yz(y^3 + 8yz) = yz \cdot y(y^2 + 8z) = y^2z(y^2 + 8z)\]

So our inequality becomes:
\[y^2|y^4 - 48z + 20z^2| \geq y^2z(y^2 + 8z)\]

Since $y = 2^x > 0$, we can divide by $y^2$:
\[|y^4 - 48z + 20z^2| \geq z(y^2 + 8z)\]

Let $t = \frac{z}{y^2} = \frac{3^x}{2^{2x}} = \frac{3^x}{4^x} = \left(\frac{3}{4}\right)^x$.

Then $z = ty^2$ and our inequality becomes:
\[|y^4 - 48ty^2 + 20t^2y^4| \geq ty^2(y^2 + 8ty^2)\]
\[y^2|y^2 - 48t + 20t^2y^2| \geq ty^2 \cdot y^2(1 + 8t)\]
\[|y^2 - 48t + 20t^2y^2| \geq ty^2(1 + 8t)\]
\[|y^2(1 + 20t^2) - 48t| \geq ty^2(1 + 8t)\]
