\section*{Problem 1}
Ako je $k \in \mathbf{Z}$ i $0{,}0010101 \cdot 10^k > 1001$, koja je najmanja moguća vrednost za $k$?

\subsection*{Solution}
We need to find the smallest integer $k$ such that:
\[
0{,}0010101 \cdot 10^k > 1001
\]

First, let's express $0{,}0010101$ in scientific notation:
\[
0{,}0010101 = 1{,}0101 \times 10^{-3}
\]

So we need:
\[
1{,}0101 \times 10^{-3} \cdot 10^k > 1001
\]
\[
1{,}0101 \times 10^{k-3} > 1001
\]

Since $1001 \approx 10^3$, we need:
\[
10^{k-3} > \frac{1001}{1{,}0101} \approx 991
\]

For $k-3 = 2$: $10^2 = 100 < 991$ — not sufficient.

For $k-3 = 3$: $10^3 = 1000 > 991$ — sufficient.

Let's verify with $k = 6$:
\[
0{,}0010101 \cdot 10^6 = 1010{,}1 > 1001 \checkmark
\]

Let's check $k = 5$:
\[
0{,}0010101 \cdot 10^5 = 101{,}01 < 1001
\]

Therefore, the smallest value is $k = 6$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$6$ (option \textbf{D}).
