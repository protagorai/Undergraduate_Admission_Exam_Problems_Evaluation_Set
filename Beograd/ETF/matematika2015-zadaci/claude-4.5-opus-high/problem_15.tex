\section*{Problem 15}
Skup svih realnih vrednosti $x$ za koje va≈æi nejednakost $|4^{3x} - 2^{4x+2} \cdot 3^{x+1} + 20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x| \geq 8 \cdot 6^x(8^{x-1} + 6^x)$ je oblika (za neke realne brojeve $a, b, c$ i $d$ takve da je $-\infty < a < b < c < d < +\infty$):

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's simplify using substitutions. Let $u = 2^x$ and $v = 3^x$.

Left side:
\[
4^{3x} = 2^{6x} = u^6
\]
\[
2^{4x+2} \cdot 3^{x+1} = 4 \cdot 2^{4x} \cdot 3 \cdot 3^x = 12u^4v
\]
\[
20 \cdot 12^x \cdot 3^x = 20 \cdot (2^2 \cdot 3)^x \cdot 3^x = 20u^2v \cdot v = 20u^2v^2
\]

So LHS $= |u^6 - 12u^4v + 20u^2v^2|$

Right side:
\[
8 \cdot 6^x(8^{x-1} + 6^x) = 8 \cdot 2^x \cdot 3^x \left(\frac{2^{3x}}{8} + 2^x \cdot 3^x\right) = 8uv\left(\frac{u^3}{8} + uv\right)
\]
\[
= 8uv \cdot \frac{u^3}{8} + 8uv \cdot uv = u^4v + 8u^2v^2
\]

So the inequality becomes:
\[
|u^6 - 12u^4v + 20u^2v^2| \geq u^4v + 8u^2v^2
\]

Divide by $u^2v^2$ (positive):
\[
\left|\frac{u^4}{v^2} - 12\frac{u^2}{v} + 20\right| \geq \frac{u^2}{v} + 8
\]

Let $t = \dfrac{u^2}{v} = \dfrac{2^{2x}}{3^x} = \left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right)^x \cdot 3^{x-x} = \left(\dfrac{4}{3}\right)^x$.

So we have:
\[
|t^2 - 12t + 20| \geq t + 8
\]

Factor: $t^2 - 12t + 20 = (t-2)(t-10)$

\textbf{Case 1:} $t \leq 2$ or $t \geq 10$: $(t-2)(t-10) \geq 0$
\[
t^2 - 12t + 20 \geq t + 8
\]
\[
t^2 - 13t + 12 \geq 0
\]
\[
(t-1)(t-12) \geq 0
\]
So $t \leq 1$ or $t \geq 12$.

Combined with $t \leq 2$ or $t \geq 10$: $t \leq 1$ or $t \geq 12$.

\textbf{Case 2:} $2 < t < 10$: $(t-2)(t-10) < 0$
\[
-(t^2 - 12t + 20) \geq t + 8
\]
\[
-t^2 + 12t - 20 \geq t + 8
\]
\[
-t^2 + 11t - 28 \geq 0
\]
\[
t^2 - 11t + 28 \leq 0
\]
\[
(t-4)(t-7) \leq 0
\]
So $4 \leq t \leq 7$.

Combined with $2 < t < 10$: $4 \leq t \leq 7$.

Overall: $t \leq 1$ or $4 \leq t \leq 7$ or $t \geq 12$.

Since $t = (4/3)^x$: $t \leq 1 \Leftrightarrow x \leq 0$, $t = 4 \Leftrightarrow x = \log_{4/3}4$, etc.

The solution is $(-\infty, a] \cup [b, c] \cup [d, +\infty)$ form, which matches option \textbf{A}: $(-\infty, a] \cup [b, c] \cup [d, +\infty)$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$(-\infty, a] \cup [b, c] \cup [d, +\infty)$ (option \textbf{A}).
