\section*{Problem 2}
Vrednost izraza $[(1+\frac{1}{2})^{-1} : (1+\frac{1}{3})]^{-2} \cdot (1+\frac{1}{4})$ jednaka je:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[A)] $5$
    \item[B)] $0.2$
    \item[C)] $1$
    \item[D)] $0.5$
    \item[E)] $3$
    \item[N)] Ne znam
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Solution}
Calculate the terms inside the brackets first:
\[ 1 + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2} \implies \left(1 + \frac{1}{2}\right)^{-1} = \frac{2}{3}. \]
\[ 1 + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{4}{3}. \]
Now perform the division:
\[ \left(1+\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-1} : \left(1+\frac{1}{3}\right) = \frac{2}{3} : \frac{4}{3} = \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{4} = \frac{2}{4} = \frac{1}{2}. \]
Raise to the power of $-2$:
\[ \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-2} = \left(\frac{2}{1}\right)^2 = 4. \]
Finally, multiply by the last term:
\[ 1 + \frac{1}{4} = \frac{5}{4}. \]
\[ 4 \cdot \frac{5}{4} = 5. \]

\subsection*{Answer}
The value is $5$, which corresponds to option \textbf{A}.
