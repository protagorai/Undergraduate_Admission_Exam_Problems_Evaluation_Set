\section*{Problem 9}
Stranica romba čija je površina $80 \text{cm}^2$, a odnos dijagonala $4 : 5$, iznosi (u cm):
\begin{itemize}
    \item[A)] $\sqrt{84}$
    \item[B)] $\sqrt{81}$
    \item[C)] $\sqrt{72}$
    \item[D)] $\sqrt{80}$
    \item[E)] $\sqrt{82}$
    \item[N)] Ne znam
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the diagonals be $d_1 = 4k$ and $d_2 = 5k$.
The area of a rhombus is given by $P = \frac{d_1 d_2}{2}$.
\[ 80 = \frac{4k \cdot 5k}{2} = \frac{20k^2}{2} = 10k^2. \]
\[ k^2 = 8 \implies k = \sqrt{8} = 2\sqrt{2}. \]
The diagonals are:
\[ d_1 = 4(2\sqrt{2}) = 8\sqrt{2}, \quad d_2 = 5(2\sqrt{2}) = 10\sqrt{2}. \]
The side of the rhombus $a$ satisfies $a^2 = (\frac{d_1}{2})^2 + (\frac{d_2}{2})^2$.
\[ a^2 = (4\sqrt{2})^2 + (5\sqrt{2})^2 = 16 \cdot 2 + 25 \cdot 2 = 32 + 50 = 82. \]
\[ a = \sqrt{82}. \]

\subsection*{Answer}
The side length is $\sqrt{82}$, which corresponds to option \textbf{E}.
