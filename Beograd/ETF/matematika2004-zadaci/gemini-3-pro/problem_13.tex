\section*{Problem 13}
Sistem jednačina $3^x - 2^{y^2} = 77$, $3^{\frac{x}{2}} - 2^{\frac{y^2}{2}} = 7$ ima:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[A)] jedno realno rešenje
    \item[B)] dva realna rešenja
    \item[C)] četiri realna rešenja
    \item[D)] tri realna rešenja
    \item[E)] prazan skup realnih rešenja
    \item[N)] Ne znam
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $u = 3^{x/2}$ and $v = 2^{y^2/2}$. Since they are exponential functions, $u > 0$ and $v > 0$.
The system becomes:
\begin{align*}
u^2 - v^2 &= 77 \\
u - v &= 7
\end{align*}
Factor the first equation:
\[ (u-v)(u+v) = 77. \]
Substitute $u-v=7$:
\[ 7(u+v) = 77 \implies u+v = 11. \]
Now we have a linear system for $u$ and $v$:
\begin{align*}
u - v &= 7 \\
u + v &= 11
\end{align*}
Adding the equations: $2u = 18 \implies u = 9$.
Subtracting the equations: $2v = 4 \implies v = 2$.
Now substitute back to find $x$ and $y$:
\[ 3^{x/2} = 9 = 3^2 \implies \frac{x}{2} = 2 \implies x = 4. \]
\[ 2^{y^2/2} = 2 = 2^1 \implies \frac{y^2}{2} = 1 \implies y^2 = 2 \implies y = \pm \sqrt{2}. \]
The solutions for $(x, y)$ are $(4, \sqrt{2})$ and $(4, -\sqrt{2})$.
Thus, there are two real solutions.

\subsection*{Answer}
The system has two real solutions, which corresponds to option \textbf{B}.
