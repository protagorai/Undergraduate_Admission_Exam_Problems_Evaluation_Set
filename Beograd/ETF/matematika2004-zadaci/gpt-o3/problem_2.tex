\section*{Problem 2}
Vrednost izraza $\bigl[(1+\tfrac12)^{-1}:(1+\tfrac13)\bigr]^{-2}\cdot(1+\tfrac14)$ jednaka je:
A) $5$\; B) $0{,}2$\; C) $1$\; D) $0{,}5$\; E) $3$\; N) Ne znam.

\subsection*{Solution}
Najpre
\[1+\tfrac12=\tfrac32,\qquad(1+\tfrac12)^{-1}=\tfrac23,\qquad1+\tfrac13=\tfrac43.\]
Sledi
\[\frac{2}{3}:\frac{4}{3}=\frac23\cdot\frac34=\frac12.\]
Potenciрамо на $-2$:
\[\left(\frac12\right)^{-2}=\left(\frac21\right)^2=4.\]
Zatim
\[1+\tfrac14=\tfrac54,\qquad4\cdot\tfrac54=5.\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$5$ (opcija \textbf{A}).
