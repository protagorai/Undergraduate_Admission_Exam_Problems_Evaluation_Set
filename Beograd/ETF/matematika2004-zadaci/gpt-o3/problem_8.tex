\section*{Problem 8}
ReÅ¡iti $\log_{b^2}x+\log_{x^2}b=1$ uz uslov $b>0,\;b\ne1,\;x\ne1$. Dati odgovori:
A) $\tfrac1b$\; B) $\tfrac1{b^2}$\; C) $b$\; D) $b^2$\; E) $\sqrt b$\; N) Ne znam.

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo formulu $\log_{a^k}m=\frac1k\log_am$:
\[\log_{b^2}x=\frac12\log_bx,\qquad\log_{x^2}b=\frac12\log_xb.\]
Uslov postaje $\tfrac12(\log_bx+\log_xb)=1\implies\log_bx+\log_xb=2$.
Stavimo $t=\log_bx\;(\ne0)$, tada je $\log_xb=\tfrac1t$.
\[t+\frac1t=2\;\Longrightarrow\;t^2-2t+1=0\Longrightarrow(t-1)^2=0\Longrightarrow t=1.\]
Dakle $x=b^1=b$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$b$ (opcija \textbf{C}).
