\section*{Problem 17}
Dati su izrazi
\[
E_1=\sin^2\frac{x+y}{2}+\cos x\cos y,\quad
E_2=\cos^2\frac{x-y}{2}-\sin x\sin y,
\]
\[
E_3=\cos^2\frac{x+y}{2}+\sin x\sin y,\quad
E_4=\sin^2\frac{x-y}{2}+\cos x\cos y.
\]
Odrediti taƒçan iskaz o jednakosti ovih izraza.

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo formule
\[
\sin^2\frac{u}{2}=\frac{1-\cos u}{2},\qquad \cos^2\frac{u}{2}=\frac{1+\cos u}{2},
\]
\[
\cos x\cos y=\frac{\cos(x-y)+\cos(x+y)}{2},\qquad
\sin x\sin y=\frac{\cos(x-y)-\cos(x+y)}{2}.
\]

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{1) $E_1$.}
\[
E_1=\frac{1-\cos(x+y)}{2}+\frac{\cos(x-y)+\cos(x+y)}{2}
=\frac{1+\cos(x-y)}{2}=\cos^2\frac{x-y}{2}.
\]

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{2) $E_2$.}
\[
E_2=\frac{1+\cos(x-y)}{2}-\frac{\cos(x-y)-\cos(x+y)}{2}
=\frac{1+\cos(x+y)}{2}=\cos^2\frac{x+y}{2}.
\]

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{3) $E_3$.}
\[
E_3=\frac{1+\cos(x+y)}{2}+\frac{\cos(x-y)-\cos(x+y)}{2}
=\frac{1+\cos(x-y)}{2}=\cos^2\frac{x-y}{2}.
\]

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{4) $E_4$.}
\[
E_4=\frac{1-\cos(x-y)}{2}+\frac{\cos(x-y)+\cos(x+y)}{2}
=\frac{1+\cos(x+y)}{2}=\cos^2\frac{x+y}{2}.
\]

Dakle, $E_1=E_3$ i $E_2=E_4$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$E_1=E_3,\ E_2=E_4$ (option \textbf{E}).
