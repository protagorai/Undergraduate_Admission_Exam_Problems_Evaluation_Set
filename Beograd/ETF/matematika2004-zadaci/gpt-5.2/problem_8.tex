\section*{Problem 8}
Ako je
\[
\log_{b^2}x+\log_{x^2}b=1,\qquad b>0,\ b\ne 1,\ x>0,\ x\ne 1,
\]
odrediti $x$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Prebacimo sve na logaritme sa osnovom $b$ i $x$:
\[
\log_{b^2}x=\frac{\ln x}{\ln b^2}=\frac{\ln x}{2\ln b}=\frac12\log_b x,
\qquad
\log_{x^2}b=\frac{\ln b}{\ln x^2}=\frac{\ln b}{2\ln x}=\frac12\log_x b.
\]
Jednačina postaje
\[
\frac12\big(\log_b x+\log_x b\big)=1
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\log_b x+\log_x b=2.
\]
Označimo $u=\log_b x$. Tada je $\log_x b=\frac1u$, pa dobijamo
\[
u+\frac1u=2
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
u^2-2u+1=0
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
(u-1)^2=0,
\]
odakle je $u=1$, tj.
\[
\log_b x=1 \Longleftrightarrow x=b.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$x=b$ (option \textbf{C}).
