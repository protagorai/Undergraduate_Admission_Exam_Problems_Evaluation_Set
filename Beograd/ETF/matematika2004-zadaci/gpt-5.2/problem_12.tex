\section*{Problem 12}
Data je funkcija $f(x)=2x-x^2$. Odrediti $f\bigl(f(f(1-x))\bigr)$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Najpre izraƒçunamo redom:
\[
f(1-x)=2(1-x)-(1-x)^2
=2-2x-(1-2x+x^2)=1-x^2.
\]
Zatim
\[
f(f(1-x))=f(1-x^2)=2(1-x^2)-(1-x^2)^2
=2-2x^2-(1-2x^2+x^4)=1-x^4.
\]
Najzad,
\[
f(f(f(1-x)))=f(1-x^4)=2(1-x^4)-(1-x^4)^2
=2-2x^4-(1-2x^4+x^8)=1-x^8.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$1-x^8$ (option \textbf{E}).
