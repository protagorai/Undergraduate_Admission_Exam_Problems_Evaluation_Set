\section*{Problem 6}
Neka su $\alpha$, $\beta$ i $\gamma$ uglovi a $a$, $b$, $c$ stranice trougla. Tada je $a\sin(\beta-\gamma) + b\sin(\gamma-\alpha) + c\sin(\alpha-\beta)$ jednako:

A) $2\cos(\alpha+\beta-\gamma)$; \quad B) $\cos(\alpha-\beta-\gamma)$; \quad C) $1$; \quad D) $0$; \quad E) $-1$; \quad N) Ne znam.

\subsection*{Rešenje}
Koristimo sinusnu teoremu: $\frac{a}{\sin\alpha} = \frac{b}{\sin\beta} = \frac{c}{\sin\gamma} = 2R$, gde je $R$ poluprečnik opisanog kruga.

Dakle: $a = 2R\sin\alpha$, $b = 2R\sin\beta$, $c = 2R\sin\gamma$.

Zamenom:
\begin{align*}
&a\sin(\beta-\gamma) + b\sin(\gamma-\alpha) + c\sin(\alpha-\beta)\\
&= 2R[\sin\alpha\sin(\beta-\gamma) + \sin\beta\sin(\gamma-\alpha) + \sin\gamma\sin(\alpha-\beta)]
\end{align*}

Koristimo formulu: $\sin A \sin B = \frac{1}{2}[\cos(A-B) - \cos(A+B)]$

\begin{align*}
\sin\alpha\sin(\beta-\gamma) &= \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\alpha-\beta+\gamma) - \cos(\alpha+\beta-\gamma)]\\
\sin\beta\sin(\gamma-\alpha) &= \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\beta-\gamma+\alpha) - \cos(\beta+\gamma-\alpha)]\\
\sin\gamma\sin(\alpha-\beta) &= \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\gamma-\alpha+\beta) - \cos(\gamma+\alpha-\beta)]
\end{align*}

Pošto je $\alpha + \beta + \gamma = \pi$:
\begin{align*}
\alpha+\beta-\gamma &= \pi - 2\gamma\\
\beta+\gamma-\alpha &= \pi - 2\alpha\\
\gamma+\alpha-\beta &= \pi - 2\beta
\end{align*}

Zbir postaje:
\begin{align*}
&\frac{1}{2}[\cos(\alpha-\beta+\gamma) + \cos(\beta-\gamma+\alpha) + \cos(\gamma-\alpha+\beta)]\\
&- \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\pi-2\gamma) + \cos(\pi-2\alpha) + \cos(\pi-2\beta)]
\end{align*}

Primetimo da su $\alpha-\beta+\gamma = \pi - 2\beta$, $\beta-\gamma+\alpha = \pi - 2\gamma$, $\gamma-\alpha+\beta = \pi - 2\alpha$.

Tako da se oba dela poništavaju, i dobijamo:
\[
2R \cdot 0 = 0
\]

\subsection*{Odgovor}
$0$ (opcija \textbf{D}).
