\section*{Problem 2}
Vrednost izraza $[(1+\frac{1}{2})^{-1} : (1+\frac{1}{3})]^{-2} \cdot (1+\frac{1}{4})$ jednaka je:

A) $5$; \quad B) $0.2$; \quad C) $1$; \quad D) $0.5$; \quad E) $3$; \quad N) Ne znam.

\subsection*{Rešenje}
Izračunajmo redom:
\begin{align*}
1 + \frac{1}{2} &= \frac{3}{2}\\
1 + \frac{1}{3} &= \frac{4}{3}\\
1 + \frac{1}{4} &= \frac{5}{4}
\end{align*}

Sada:
\[
\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{-1} = \frac{2}{3}
\]

Zatim:
\[
\frac{2}{3} : \frac{4}{3} = \frac{2}{3} \cdot \frac{3}{4} = \frac{2}{4} = \frac{1}{2}
\]

Dalje:
\[
\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{-2} = 2^2 = 4
\]

Konačno:
\[
4 \cdot \frac{5}{4} = 5
\]

\subsection*{Odgovor}
$5$ (opcija \textbf{A}).
