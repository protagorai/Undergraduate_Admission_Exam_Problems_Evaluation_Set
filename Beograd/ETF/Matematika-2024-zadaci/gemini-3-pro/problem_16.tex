\section*{Problem 16}
Graniƒçna vrednost $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^{\sin^2 x} - \cos x}{\sqrt{x^2+1}-1}$ iznosi:

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo Tejlorove razvoje funkcija u okolini nule:
$\sin x \approx x - \frac{x^3}{6}$, pa $\sin^2 x \approx x^2$.
$e^t \approx 1 + t$, pa $e^{\sin^2 x} \approx 1 + x^2$.
$\cos x \approx 1 - \frac{x^2}{2}$.
$\sqrt{1+t} \approx 1 + \frac{t}{2}$, pa $\sqrt{1+x^2} \approx 1 + \frac{x^2}{2}$.

Zamenimo u limes:
Brojilac:
$e^{\sin^2 x} - \cos x \approx (1 + x^2) - (1 - \frac{x^2}{2}) = x^2 + \frac{x^2}{2} = \frac{3}{2}x^2$.

Imenilac:
$\sqrt{x^2+1} - 1 \approx (1 + \frac{x^2}{2}) - 1 = \frac{x^2}{2}$.

Limes postaje:
$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{3}{2}x^2}{\frac{1}{2}x^2} = \frac{3/2}{1/2} = 3$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$3$ (option \textbf{D}).
