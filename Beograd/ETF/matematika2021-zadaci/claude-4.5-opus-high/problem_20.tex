\section*{Problem 20}
Grani훾na vrednost $\displaystyle\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x \cos(2x) \cos(3x)}{1 - \cos x}$ jednaka je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo Tejlorov razvoj za $\cos u \approx 1 - \frac{u^2}{2} + O(u^4)$ za malo $u$.

\[
\cos x \approx 1 - \frac{x^2}{2}
\]
\[
\cos(2x) \approx 1 - \frac{4x^2}{2} = 1 - 2x^2
\]
\[
\cos(3x) \approx 1 - \frac{9x^2}{2}
\]

Proizvod:
\[
\cos x \cos(2x) \cos(3x) \approx \left(1 - \frac{x^2}{2}\right)\left(1 - 2x^2\right)\left(1 - \frac{9x^2}{2}\right)
\]

Zadr탑avamo samo 훾lanove do $x^2$:
\[
\approx 1 - \frac{x^2}{2} - 2x^2 - \frac{9x^2}{2} + O(x^4)
\]
\[
= 1 - \left(\frac{1}{2} + 2 + \frac{9}{2}\right)x^2 = 1 - 7x^2
\]

Brojilac:
\[
1 - \cos x \cos(2x) \cos(3x) \approx 7x^2
\]

Imenilac:
\[
1 - \cos x \approx \frac{x^2}{2}
\]

Grani훾na vrednost:
\[
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{7x^2}{\frac{x^2}{2}} = \frac{7x^2 \cdot 2}{x^2} = 14
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$14$ (option \textbf{B}).
