\section*{Problem 1}
A student reads a book for 20 days, reading 45 minutes each day. How many days would it take to read the same book if reading for 1 hour daily?

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's calculate the total reading time needed:
\[
\text{Total time} = 20 \text{ days} \times 45 \text{ minutes/day} = 900 \text{ minutes}
\]

If reading 1 hour (60 minutes) per day, the number of days needed is:
\[
\text{Days needed} = \frac{900 \text{ minutes}}{60 \text{ minutes/day}} = 15 \text{ days}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
15 days (option \textbf{E}).
