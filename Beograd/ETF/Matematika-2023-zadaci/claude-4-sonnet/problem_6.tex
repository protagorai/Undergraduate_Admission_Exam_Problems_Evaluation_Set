\section*{Problem 6}
Ako su $x_1$, $x_2$, $x_3$ koreni jednačine $px^3 + qx^2 + px + 1 = 0$, $(p, q \in \mathbb{R}$ i $p \neq 0)$, tada vrednost izraza $\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + \frac{1}{x_3}$ iznosi:

\subsection*{Solution}
Imamo polinom $P(x) = px^3 + qx^2 + px + 1 = 0$ sa korenima $x_1$, $x_2$, $x_3$.

Prema Vieta-ovim formulama za polinom $ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d = 0$:
\begin{align}
x_1 + x_2 + x_3 &= -\frac{b}{a}\\
x_1x_2 + x_1x_3 + x_2x_3 &= \frac{c}{a}\\
x_1x_2x_3 &= -\frac{d}{a}
\end{align}

Za naš polinom $px^3 + qx^2 + px + 1 = 0$:
\begin{align}
x_1 + x_2 + x_3 &= -\frac{q}{p}\\
x_1x_2 + x_1x_3 + x_2x_3 &= \frac{p}{p} = 1\\
x_1x_2x_3 &= -\frac{1}{p}
\end{align}

Tražimo:
\[
\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + \frac{1}{x_3} = \frac{x_2x_3 + x_1x_3 + x_1x_2}{x_1x_2x_3}
\]

Iz Vieta-ovih formula:
\[
\frac{1}{x_1} + \frac{1}{x_2} + \frac{1}{x_3} = \frac{x_1x_2 + x_1x_3 + x_2x_3}{x_1x_2x_3} = \frac{1}{-\frac{1}{p}} = \frac{1 \cdot p}{-1} = -p
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-p$ (opcija \textbf{E}).
