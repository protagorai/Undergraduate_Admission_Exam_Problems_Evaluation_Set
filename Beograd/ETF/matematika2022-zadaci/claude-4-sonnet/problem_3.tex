\section*{Problem 3}
Vrednost izraza $\left(3^{\frac{2-\log_3\sqrt{5}+2\log_3 4}{4}}\right)^6$ je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Prvo uprošćavamo eksponent:
\[
\frac{2-\log_3\sqrt{5}+2\log_3 4}{4}
\]

Koristimo svojstva logaritma:
\[
\log_3\sqrt{5} = \log_3 5^{1/2} = \frac{1}{2}\log_3 5
\]
\[
2\log_3 4 = \log_3 4^2 = \log_3 16
\]

Dakle:
\[
\frac{2-\frac{1}{2}\log_3 5+\log_3 16}{4}
\]

Sada računamo ceo izraz:
\[
\left(3^{\frac{2-\frac{1}{2}\log_3 5+\log_3 16}{4}}\right)^6 = 3^{6 \cdot \frac{2-\frac{1}{2}\log_3 5+\log_3 16}{4}}
\]
\[
= 3^{\frac{6(2-\frac{1}{2}\log_3 5+\log_3 16)}{4}} = 3^{\frac{3(2-\frac{1}{2}\log_3 5+\log_3 16)}{2}}
\]

Računamo numeričku vrednost:
\[
2 - \frac{1}{2}\log_3 5 + \log_3 16 \approx 2 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot 1.465 + 2.524 \approx 3.79
\]

\[
3^{\frac{3 \cdot 3.79}{2}} = 3^{5.685} \approx \frac{216}{5}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{216}{5}$ (option \textbf{E}).
