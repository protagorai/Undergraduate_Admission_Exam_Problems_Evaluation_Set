\section*{Problem 1}
The price was $100000$ dinars, then increased by $25\%$. After that, the new price was decreased by $20\%$. Find the final price.

\subsection*{Solution}
After the $25\%$ increase:
\[
100000\cdot 1.25 = 125000.
\]
After the $20\%$ decrease:
\[
125000\cdot 0.8 = 100000.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$100000$ (option \textbf{E}).
