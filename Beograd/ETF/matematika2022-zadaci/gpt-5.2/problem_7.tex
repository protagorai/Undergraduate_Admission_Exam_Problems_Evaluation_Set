\section*{Problem 7}
Compute
\[
\lim_{x\to \pi/4}\frac{\cot^3 x-1}{2-\cot x-\cot^3 x}.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $u=\cot x$. As $x\to\pi/4$ we have $u\to 1$. The limit becomes
\[
\lim_{u\to 1}\frac{u^3-1}{2-u-u^3}.
\]
Factor:
\[
u^3-1=(u-1)(u^2+u+1),
\]
and
\[
2-u-u^3=-(u^3+u-2)=-(u-1)(u^2+u+2).
\]
Cancel $(u-1)$:
\[
\lim_{u\to 1}-\frac{u^2+u+1}{u^2+u+2}
=-\frac{1+1+1}{1+1+2}=-\frac{3}{4}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\dfrac{3}{4}$ (option \textbf{B}).
