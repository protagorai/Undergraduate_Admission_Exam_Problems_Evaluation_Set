\section*{Problem 5}
Let
\[
P(x)=x^{2022}-2x^{2021}+x^{2019}-x^2+2x+1,\qquad Q(x)=x^2+1.
\]
Find the remainder when $P(x)$ is divided by $Q(x)$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Modulo $x^2+1$ we have $x^2\equiv -1$. Hence
\[
x^{2k}\equiv (-1)^k,\qquad x^{2k+1}\equiv (-1)^k x.
\]
Compute each term:
\[
x^{2022}=x^{2\cdot 1011}\equiv (-1)^{1011}=-1,
\]
\[
-2x^{2021}=-2x^{2\cdot 1010+1}\equiv -2(-1)^{1010}x=-2x,
\]
\[
x^{2019}=x^{2\cdot 1009+1}\equiv (-1)^{1009}x=-x.
\]
Also,
\[
-x^2+2x+1\equiv -(-1)+2x+1=2+2x.
\]
Summing:
\[
(-1)+(-2x)+(-x)+(2+2x)=1-x.
\]
So the remainder is $1-x=-x+1$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$-x+1$ (option \textbf{E}).
