\section*{Problem 15}
Zbir realnih rešenja jednačine $\text{tg}(x - \frac{\pi}{12}) \text{ctg}(x + \frac{\pi}{12}) = \frac{1}{3}$ na segmentu $[0, 2\pi]$ iznosi:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[(A)] $3\pi$
    \item[(B)] $\pi/4$
    \item[(C)] $5\pi/4$
    \item[(D)] $4\pi$
    \item[(E)] $3\pi/2$
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Solution}
Jednačinu zapisujemo kao:
\[ \frac{\sin(x - \pi/12)}{\cos(x - \pi/12)} \cdot \frac{\cos(x + \pi/12)}{\sin(x + \pi/12)} = \frac{1}{3} \]
\[ 3 \sin(x - \pi/12) \cos(x + \pi/12) = \cos(x - \pi/12) \sin(x + \pi/12) \]
Koristimo formule za pretvaranje proizvoda u zbir:
\[ 3 \cdot \frac{1}{2}[\sin(2x) + \sin(-\pi/6)] = \frac{1}{2}[\sin(2x) + \sin(\pi/6)] \]
\[ 3(\sin 2x - 1/2) = \sin 2x + 1/2 \]
\[ 3\sin 2x - 3/2 = \sin 2x + 1/2 \]
\[ 2\sin 2x = 2 \implies \sin 2x = 1 \]
Rešenja su $2x = \pi/2 + 2k\pi$, tj. $x = \pi/4 + k\pi$.
Na intervalu $[0, 2\pi]$:
Za $k=0$, $x_1 = \pi/4$.
Za $k=1$, $x_2 = 5\pi/4$.
Provera uslova definisanosti (imenilac različit od nule):
$\cos(x - \pi/12) \neq 0$: Za $\pi/4$, $\cos(\pi/6) \neq 0$. OK.
$\sin(x + \pi/12) \neq 0$: Za $\pi/4$, $\sin(\pi/3) \neq 0$. OK.
Zbir rešenja: $x_1 + x_2 = \pi/4 + 5\pi/4 = 6\pi/4 = 3\pi/2$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$3\pi/2$ (option \textbf{E}).
