\section*{Problem 3}
Vrednost izraza $\left( 3^{\frac{2 - \log_{3\sqrt{3}} 5 + 2\log_9 4}{4}} \right)^6$ je:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[(A)] 1
    \item[(B)] 8/9
    \item[(C)] 2
    \item[(D)] 25/9
    \item[(E)] 216/5
    \item[(N)] Ne znam
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{Solution}
Oznaczimo izraz sa $I$.
\[ I = 3^{\frac{6}{4} (2 - \log_{3\sqrt{3}} 5 + 2\log_9 4)} = 3^{\frac{3}{2} (2 - \log_{3\sqrt{3}} 5 + 2\log_9 4)} \]
Sredimo logaritme u eksponentu.
Osnova prvog logaritma: $3\sqrt{3} = 3^1 \cdot 3^{1/2} = 3^{3/2}$.
\[ \log_{3\sqrt{3}} 5 = \log_{3^{3/2}} 5 = \frac{1}{3/2} \log_3 5 = \frac{2}{3} \log_3 5 \]
Drugi logaritam:
\[ 2\log_9 4 = 2\log_{3^2} 2^2 = 2 \cdot \frac{2}{2} \log_3 2 = 2 \log_3 2 \]
Zamenimo u eksponent:
\[ E = \frac{3}{2} \left( 2 - \frac{2}{3} \log_3 5 + 2 \log_3 2 \right) = 3 - \log_3 5 + 3 \log_3 2 \]
Sada raƒçunamo vrednost izraza:
\[ I = 3^{3 - \log_3 5 + 3 \log_3 2} = \frac{3^3 \cdot 3^{3 \log_3 2}}{3^{\log_3 5}} \]
\[ I = \frac{27 \cdot (3^{\log_3 2})^3}{5} = \frac{27 \cdot 2^3}{5} = \frac{27 \cdot 8}{5} = \frac{216}{5} \]

\subsection*{Answer}
216/5 (option \textbf{E}).
