\section*{Problem 5}
Zbir
\[
\sin\frac{3\pi}{7}+\sin\frac{4\pi}{7}
\]
jednak je:
\[
\text{(A)}~-2\sin\frac{\pi}{14}\quad
\text{(B)}~-2\cos\frac{\pi}{14}\quad
\text{(C)}~2\sin\frac{\pi}{14}\quad
\text{(D)}~2\cos\frac{\pi}{7}\quad
\text{(E)}~2\cos\frac{\pi}{14}.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Primeni formulu $\sin A+\sin B=2\sin\frac{A+B}{2}\cos\frac{A-B}{2}$:
\[
\sin\frac{3\pi}{7}+\sin\frac{4\pi}{7}
=2\sin\frac{\frac{3\pi}{7}+\frac{4\pi}{7}}{2}\cos\frac{\frac{3\pi}{7}-\frac{4\pi}{7}}{2}
=2\sin\frac{\pi}{2}\cos\left(-\frac{\pi}{14}\right).
\]
Po≈°to je $\sin\frac{\pi}{2}=1$ i $\cos$ parna funkcija, dobijamo
\[
2\cos\frac{\pi}{14}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$2\cos\frac{\pi}{14}$ (option \textbf{E}).
