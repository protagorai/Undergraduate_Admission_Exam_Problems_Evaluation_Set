\section*{Problem 7}
Ako su $x_1$ i $x_2$ koreni kvadratne jedna훾ine
\[
\frac{1}{x-1}+\frac{1}{x-2}=1,
\]
tada je izraz
\[
\frac{x_1}{x_2}+\frac{x_2}{x_1}
\]
jednak:
\[
\text{(A)}~\frac13\quad
\text{(B)}~\frac23\quad
\text{(C)}~2\quad
\text{(D)}~3\quad
\text{(E)}~5.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Prebacimo na zajedni훾ki imenitelj:
\[
\frac{(x-2)+(x-1)}{(x-1)(x-2)}=1
\;\Rightarrow\;
\frac{2x-3}{(x-1)(x-2)}=1.
\]
Dakle,
\[
2x-3=(x-1)(x-2)=x^2-3x+2
\;\Rightarrow\;
x^2-5x+5=0.
\]
Za korene va탑e Vijetove formule:
\[
x_1+x_2=5,\qquad x_1x_2=5.
\]
Tra탑eni izraz je
\[
\frac{x_1}{x_2}+\frac{x_2}{x_1}=\frac{x_1^2+x_2^2}{x_1x_2}
=\frac{(x_1+x_2)^2-2x_1x_2}{x_1x_2}
=\frac{25-10}{5}=3.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$3$ (option \textbf{D}).
