\section*{Problem 1}
Koji od pet datih izraza ima različitu vrednost od ostala četiri izraza?

(A) $2^8$ \quad (B) $4^4$ \quad (C) $8^{8/3}$ \quad (D) $16^2$ \quad (E) $32^{6/5}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's calculate each expression:

(A) $2^8 = 256$

(B) $4^4 = (2^2)^4 = 2^8 = 256$

(C) $8^{8/3} = (2^3)^{8/3} = 2^{3 \cdot 8/3} = 2^8 = 256$

(D) $16^2 = (2^4)^2 = 2^8 = 256$

(E) $32^{6/5} = (2^5)^{6/5} = 2^{5 \cdot 6/5} = 2^6 = 64$

All expressions except (E) equal $2^8 = 256$, while (E) equals $2^6 = 64$.

\subsection*{Answer}
(E) $32^{6/5}$
