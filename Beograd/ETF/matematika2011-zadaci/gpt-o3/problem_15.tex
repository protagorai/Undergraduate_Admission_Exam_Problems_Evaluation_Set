\begin{solution}
\textbf{Zadatak 15.}

Jednačina
\[
\sqrt{\sin^{2}x+\tfrac12}+\sqrt{\cos^{2}x+\tfrac12}=2,\qquad x\in[0,2\pi]
\]
uz uslov $\sin^{2}x+\cos^{2}x=1$.

Postavimo $s=\sin^{2}x\;(0\le s\le1)$, tada je $1-s=\cos^{2}x$.  Definišimo
\[
F(s)=\sqrt{s+\tfrac12}+\sqrt{1-s+\tfrac12}.
\]
Potrebno je $F(s)=2$.

Kvadriranjem uslova $F(s)=2$ dobijamo
\[
s+\tfrac12+1-s+\tfrac12+2\sqrt{(s+\tfrac12)(1-s+\tfrac12)}=4\Longrightarrow\sqrt{(s+\tfrac12)(1.5-s)}=1.
\]
Ponovnim kvadriranjem:
\[
(s+\tfrac12)(1.5-s)=1\Longrightarrow -s^{2}+s-0.25=0\Longrightarrow (s-0.5)^{2}=0\Longrightarrow s=0.5.
\]
Dakle $\sin^{2}x=\cos^{2}x=\tfrac12\Rightarrow |\sin x|=|\cos x|=\tfrac{1}{\sqrt2}$.
Rešenja na $[0,2\pi]$ su
\[
x=\frac\pi4,\;\frac{3\pi}{4},\;\frac{5\pi}{4},\;\frac{7\pi}{4}.
\]
Njihov zbir je
\[\frac\pi4+\frac{3\pi}{4}+\frac{5\pi}{4}+\frac{7\pi}{4}=\frac{16\pi}{4}=4\pi.\]
\[
\boxed{4\pi}\;\Rightarrow\;(D).
\]
\end{solution}
