\section*{Problem 4}
Rešenje jednačine $2^{16^x} = 16^{2^x}$ jeste:

(A) $\frac{1}{2}$ \quad (B) $\frac{2}{3}$ \quad (C) $\frac{3}{4}$ \quad (D) $\frac{4}{5}$ \quad (E) $\frac{5}{6}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
We can rewrite $16 = 2^4$, so:
\[
16^{2^x} = (2^4)^{2^x} = 2^{4 \cdot 2^x}
\]

The equation becomes:
\[
2^{16^x} = 2^{4 \cdot 2^x}
\]

Since the bases are equal, the exponents must be equal:
\[
16^x = 4 \cdot 2^x
\]

We can write $16^x = (2^4)^x = 2^{4x}$, so:
\[
2^{4x} = 4 \cdot 2^x = 2^2 \cdot 2^x = 2^{x+2}
\]

Equating exponents:
\[
4x = x + 2
\]
\[
3x = 2
\]
\[
x = \frac{2}{3}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{2}{3}$ (option \textbf{B}).
