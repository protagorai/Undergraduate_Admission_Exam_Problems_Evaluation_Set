\section*{Problem 5}
Ako se zna da je polinom $x^3 + ax^2 + bx - 4$ ($a, b \in \mathbf{R}$) deljiv polinomom $x^2 - 1$, tada zbir $a^2 + b^2$ iznosi:

(A) 1 \quad (B) 17 \quad (C) 5 \quad (D) 3 \quad (E) 14 \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
If $x^3 + ax^2 + bx - 4$ is divisible by $x^2 - 1 = (x-1)(x+1)$, then $x = 1$ and $x = -1$ must be roots of the polynomial.

For $x = 1$:
\[
1 + a + b - 4 = 0 \implies a + b = 3
\]

For $x = -1$:
\[
-1 + a - b - 4 = 0 \implies a - b = 5
\]

From these two equations:
\[
a + b = 3
\]
\[
a - b = 5
\]

Adding: $2a = 8 \implies a = 4$

Subtracting: $2b = -2 \implies b = -1$

Therefore:
\[
a^2 + b^2 = 16 + 1 = 17
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$17$ (option \textbf{B}).
