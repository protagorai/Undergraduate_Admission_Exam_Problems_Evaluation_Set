\section*{Problem 3}
Ako je $f(x) = x^3 - 3x$ i $g(x) = \sin\frac{\pi}{12}x$, tada je $f(g(2))$ jednako:

(A) 0 \quad (B) $-\frac{11}{2}$ \quad (C) $\frac{11}{2}$ \quad (D) $-\frac{11}{8}$ \quad (E) $\frac{11}{8}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
First, we compute $g(2)$:
\[
g(2) = \sin\frac{\pi}{12} \cdot 2 = \sin\frac{\pi}{6} = \frac{1}{2}
\]

Now we compute $f(g(2)) = f\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)$:
\[
f\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3 - 3 \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{8} - \frac{3}{2} = \frac{1}{8} - \frac{12}{8} = -\frac{11}{8}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\frac{11}{8}$ (option \textbf{D}).
