\section*{Problem 7}
Jednačina kruga čiji centar je tačka preseka pravih $x - 2y + 4 = 0$ i $3x + y - 9 = 0$ a koji dodiruje pravu $3x + 4y + 2 = 0$ glasi:

(A) $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y - 2 = 0$ \quad (B) $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y = 0$

(C) $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y - 3 = 0$ \quad (D) $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y + 1 = 0$

(E) $x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y - 1 = 0$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
First, find the center by solving the system of equations:
\[
x - 2y + 4 = 0
\]
\[
3x + y - 9 = 0
\]

From the first equation: $x = 2y - 4$

Substituting into the second:
\[
3(2y - 4) + y - 9 = 0
\]
\[
6y - 12 + y - 9 = 0
\]
\[
7y = 21 \implies y = 3
\]
\[
x = 2(3) - 4 = 2
\]

So the center is $(2, 3)$.

The radius equals the distance from the center to the tangent line $3x + 4y + 2 = 0$:
\[
r = \frac{|3(2) + 4(3) + 2|}{\sqrt{3^2 + 4^2}} = \frac{|6 + 12 + 2|}{\sqrt{9 + 16}} = \frac{20}{5} = 4
\]

The equation of the circle with center $(2, 3)$ and radius $4$:
\[
(x - 2)^2 + (y - 3)^2 = 16
\]

Expanding:
\[
x^2 - 4x + 4 + y^2 - 6y + 9 = 16
\]
\[
x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y + 13 = 16
\]
\[
x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y - 3 = 0
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$x^2 + y^2 - 4x - 6y - 3 = 0$ (option \textbf{C}).
