\section*{Problem 9}
Zbir prvih 2012 članova aritmetičke progresije $\frac{2011}{2012}, \frac{2010}{2012}, \frac{2009}{2012}, \ldots$ iznosi:

(A) $\frac{2013}{2}$ \quad (B) $\frac{2013}{4}$ \quad (C) $\frac{2011}{4}$ \quad (D) $\frac{2011}{2}$ \quad (E) Nijedan od ponuđenih odgovora \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
The arithmetic progression has:
\begin{itemize}
    \item First term: $a_1 = \frac{2011}{2012}$
    \item Common difference: $d = \frac{2010}{2012} - \frac{2011}{2012} = -\frac{1}{2012}$
\end{itemize}

The $n$-th term is:
\[
a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d = \frac{2011}{2012} + (n-1)\left(-\frac{1}{2012}\right) = \frac{2011 - (n-1)}{2012} = \frac{2012 - n}{2012}
\]

The 2012th term is:
\[
a_{2012} = \frac{2012 - 2012}{2012} = 0
\]

The sum of the first $n$ terms of an arithmetic progression:
\[
S_n = \frac{n(a_1 + a_n)}{2}
\]

For $n = 2012$:
\[
S_{2012} = \frac{2012 \cdot \left(\frac{2011}{2012} + 0\right)}{2} = \frac{2012 \cdot \frac{2011}{2012}}{2} = \frac{2011}{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{2011}{2}$ (option \textbf{D}).
