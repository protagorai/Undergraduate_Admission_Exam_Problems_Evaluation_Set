\section*{Problem 6}
Koeficijent uz $x^{27}y^2$ u razvoju binoma $(x^3 + \sqrt{y})^{13}$ jednak je:

(A) 12 \quad (B) 1516 \quad (C) 1312 \quad (D) 715 \quad (E) 78 \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Using the binomial theorem:
\[
(x^3 + \sqrt{y})^{13} = \sum_{k=0}^{13} \binom{13}{k} (x^3)^{13-k} (\sqrt{y})^k
\]

The general term is:
\[
\binom{13}{k} x^{3(13-k)} y^{k/2}
\]

We need $x^{27}y^2$, so:
\[
3(13-k) = 27 \implies 13 - k = 9 \implies k = 4
\]

Check: $y^{k/2} = y^{4/2} = y^2$ âœ“

The coefficient is:
\[
\binom{13}{4} = \frac{13!}{4! \cdot 9!} = \frac{13 \cdot 12 \cdot 11 \cdot 10}{4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} = \frac{17160}{24} = 715
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$715$ (option \textbf{D}).
