\section*{Problem 17}
Ukupan broj realnih rešenja jednačine $3\operatorname{tg}^2 2x - 8\cos^2 x + 1 = 0$ koja pripadaju intervalu $(0, 2\pi)$ je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Rewrite the equation using $\cos 2x$.
$3\operatorname{tg}^2 2x = 3 \frac{\sin^2 2x}{\cos^2 2x} = 3 \frac{1-\cos^2 2x}{\cos^2 2x}$.
$8\cos^2 x - 1 = 4(2\cos^2 x) - 1 = 4(1+\cos 2x) - 1 = 3 + 4\cos 2x$.
The equation becomes:
\[
3 \frac{1-\cos^2 2x}{\cos^2 2x} - (3 + 4\cos 2x) = 0
\]
Let $t = \cos 2x$.
\[
3 \frac{1-t^2}{t^2} - (3+4t) = 0 \implies 3 - 3t^2 - t^2(3+4t) = 0 \implies 3 - 3t^2 - 3t^2 - 4t^3 = 0
\]
\[
4t^3 + 6t^2 - 3 = 0
\]
Let $f(t) = 4t^3 + 6t^2 - 3$. We look for roots in $[-1, 1]$ (range of cosine).
$f(-1) = -4 + 6 - 3 = -1$.
$f(1) = 7$.
$f(0) = -3$.
$f(0.5) = 4(0.125) + 6(0.25) - 3 = 0.5 + 1.5 - 3 = -1$.
$f(0.7) \approx 4(0.343) + 6(0.49) - 3 \approx 1.37 + 2.94 - 3 > 0$.
There is one root $t_0 \in (0, 1)$.
Also $f'(t) = 12t^2 + 12t = 12t(t+1)$. Roots at $0, -1$. Min at 0, Max at -1.
For $t < 0$, $f(t)$ goes from $-\infty$ up to $-1$ at $t=-1$, then down to $-3$ at $t=0$. No roots for $t \le 0$.
For $t > 0$, $f(t)$ increases from $-3$. So exactly one positive root $t_0$.
$\cos 2x = t_0$.
For $x \in (0, 2\pi)$, $2x \in (0, 4\pi)$.
The equation $\cos \theta = t_0$ (where $0 < t_0 < 1$) has 2 solutions in $[0, 2\pi)$ and 2 more in $[2\pi, 4\pi)$.
Total 4 solutions.

\subsection*{Answer}
(C) 4
