\section*{Problem 9}
Zbir prvih 2012 članova aritmetičke progresije $\frac{2011}{2012}, \frac{2010}{2012}, \frac{2009}{2012}, \dots$ iznosi:

\subsection*{Solution}
The sequence is an arithmetic progression with first term $a_1 = \frac{2011}{2012}$ and common difference $d = a_2 - a_1 = \frac{2010}{2012} - \frac{2011}{2012} = -\frac{1}{2012}$.
We need the sum $S_{2012}$.
\[
S_n = \frac{n}{2} [2a_1 + (n-1)d]
\]
For $n=2012$:
\[
S_{2012} = \frac{2012}{2} \left[2 \cdot \frac{2011}{2012} + (2012-1) \left(-\frac{1}{2012}\right)\right]
\]
\[
S_{2012} = 1006 \left[\frac{2 \cdot 2011}{2012} - \frac{2011}{2012}\right]
\]
\[
S_{2012} = 1006 \cdot \frac{2011}{2012}
\]
\[
S_{2012} = \frac{2012}{2} \cdot \frac{2011}{2012} = \frac{2011}{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(C) $\frac{2011}{2}$
