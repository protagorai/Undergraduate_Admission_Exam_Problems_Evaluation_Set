\section*{Problem 13}
Ako je $\log_2 3 = a$ i $\log_5 2 = b$, tada je $\log_{24} 50$ jednako:

\subsection*{Solution}
We express everything in base 2.
$\log_2 3 = a$.
$\log_5 2 = b \implies \log_2 5 = \frac{1}{b}$.
We need to find $\log_{24} 50 = \frac{\log_2 50}{\log_2 24}$.
Numerator:
\[
\log_2 50 = \log_2 (2 \cdot 5^2) = \log_2 2 + 2\log_2 5 = 1 + \frac{2}{b} = \frac{b+2}{b}
\]
Denominator:
\[
\log_2 24 = \log_2 (8 \cdot 3) = \log_2 2^3 + \log_2 3 = 3 + a
\]
Result:
\[
\frac{\frac{b+2}{b}}{a+3} = \frac{b+2}{b(a+3)}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(A) $\frac{b+2}{b(a+3)}$
