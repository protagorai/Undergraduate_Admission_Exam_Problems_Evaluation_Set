\section*{Problem 6}
Koeficijent uz $x^{27}y^2$ u razvoju binoma $(x^3 + \sqrt{y})^{13}$ jednak je:

\subsection*{Solution}
The general term in the expansion of $(a+b)^n$ is given by $\binom{n}{k} a^{n-k} b^k$.
Here $n=13$, $a=x^3$, $b=\sqrt{y} = y^{1/2}$.
The general term is:
\[
\binom{13}{k} (x^3)^{13-k} (y^{1/2})^k = \binom{13}{k} x^{3(13-k)} y^{k/2}
\]
We want the term with $x^{27}y^2$.
Comparing the exponents of $x$:
\[
3(13-k) = 27 \implies 13-k = 9 \implies k = 4
\]
Comparing the exponents of $y$:
\[
k/2 = 2 \implies k = 4
\]
The values of $k$ match. The coefficient is:
\[
\binom{13}{4} = \frac{13 \cdot 12 \cdot 11 \cdot 10}{4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} = 13 \cdot 11 \cdot 5 = 143 \cdot 5 = 715
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(D) 715
