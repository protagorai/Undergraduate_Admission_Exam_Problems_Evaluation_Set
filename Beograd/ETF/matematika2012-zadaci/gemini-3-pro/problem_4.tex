\section*{Problem 4}
Rešenje jednačine $2^{16^x} = 16^{2^x}$ jeste:

\subsection*{Solution}
We rewrite the bases to base 2. Note that $16 = 2^4$.
The equation becomes:
\[
2^{16^x} = (2^4)^{2^x}
\]
\[
2^{16^x} = 2^{4 \cdot 2^x}
\]
Equating exponents:
\[
16^x = 4 \cdot 2^x
\]
Substitute $16 = 2^4$ and $4 = 2^2$:
\[
(2^4)^x = 2^2 \cdot 2^x
\]
\[
2^{4x} = 2^{2+x}
\]
Equating exponents again:
\[
4x = 2 + x
\]
\[
3x = 2 \implies x = \frac{2}{3}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(B) $\frac{2}{3}$
