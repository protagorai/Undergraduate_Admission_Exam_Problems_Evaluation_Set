\section*{Problem 2}
Ukupan broj dijagonala pravilnog desetougla je:

\subsection*{Solution}
The number of diagonals $D_n$ of a regular $n$-gon is given by the formula:
\[
D_n = \frac{n(n-3)}{2}
\]
For a decagon, $n=10$:
\[
D_{10} = \frac{10(10-3)}{2} = \frac{10 \cdot 7}{2} = 5 \cdot 7 = 35
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(E) 35
