\section*{Problem 12}
Vrednost izraza $8\sin^2 80^\circ - 2\sqrt{3}\sin 40^\circ - 2\cos 40^\circ$ jednaka je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $V = 8\sin^2 80^\circ - 2\sqrt{3}\sin 40^\circ - 2\cos 40^\circ$.
Use $\sin^2 \alpha = \frac{1 - \cos 2\alpha}{2}$:
\[
8\sin^2 80^\circ = 4(1 - \cos 160^\circ) = 4 - 4\cos 160^\circ = 4 + 4\cos 20^\circ
\]
Now consider the rest:
\[
- 2\sqrt{3}\sin 40^\circ - 2\cos 40^\circ = -4 \left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\sin 40^\circ + \frac{1}{2}\cos 40^\circ\right)
\]
\[
= -4 (\sin 60^\circ \sin 40^\circ + \cos 60^\circ \cos 40^\circ) = -4 \cos(60^\circ - 40^\circ) = -4 \cos 20^\circ
\]
So:
\[
V = (4 + 4\cos 20^\circ) - 4\cos 20^\circ = 4
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(D) 4
