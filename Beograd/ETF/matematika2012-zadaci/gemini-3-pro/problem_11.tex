\section*{Problem 11}
Koja od navedenih relacija postoji između rešenja $x_1$ i $x_2$ kvadratne jednačine $x^2 - 6x + 5 + m(x^2 - 5x + 6) = 0$ ($m \in \mathbf{R}$, $m \neq -1$)?

\subsection*{Solution}
Rearrange the equation as a standard quadratic equation in $x$:
\[
x^2 - 6x + 5 + mx^2 - 5mx + 6m = 0
\]
\[
(1+m)x^2 - (6+5m)x + (5+6m) = 0
\]
Using Vieta's formulas:
\[
x_1 + x_2 = \frac{6+5m}{1+m}
\]
\[
x_1 x_2 = \frac{5+6m}{1+m}
\]
We look for a relation independent of $m$. Let's test the options.
Consider $x_1 + x_2 + x_1 x_2$:
\[
\frac{6+5m}{1+m} + \frac{5+6m}{1+m} = \frac{11 + 11m}{1+m} = \frac{11(1+m)}{1+m} = 11
\]
So $x_1 + x_2 + x_1 x_2 = 11$, or $x_1 + x_2 + x_1 x_2 - 11 = 0$.

\subsection*{Answer}
(B) $x_1 + x_2 + x_1 x_2 - 11 = 0$
