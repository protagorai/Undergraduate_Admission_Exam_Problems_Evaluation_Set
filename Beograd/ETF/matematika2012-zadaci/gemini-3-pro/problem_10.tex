\section*{Problem 10}
Ako je $a \in \mathbf{R}$ i $\left|a + \frac{1}{a}\right| = 3$, tada je $\left|a - \frac{1}{a}\right|$ jednako:

\subsection*{Solution}
We are given $\left|a + \frac{1}{a}\right| = 3$. Squaring both sides:
\[
\left(a + \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = 9 \implies a^2 + 2 + \frac{1}{a^2} = 9 \implies a^2 + \frac{1}{a^2} = 7
\]
We want to find $x = \left|a - \frac{1}{a}\right|$. Squaring $x$:
\[
x^2 = \left(a - \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = a^2 - 2 + \frac{1}{a^2}
\]
Substitute $a^2 + \frac{1}{a^2} = 7$:
\[
x^2 = 7 - 2 = 5
\]
Since $x \ge 0$, we have $x = \sqrt{5}$.

\subsection*{Answer}
(A) $\sqrt{5}$
