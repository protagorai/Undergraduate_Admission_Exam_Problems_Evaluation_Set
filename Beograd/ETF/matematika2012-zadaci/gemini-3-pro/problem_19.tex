\section*{Problem 19}
Na koliko načina se u red mogu poređati 5 učenika i 2 učenice, tako da učenice ne stoje jedna pored druge?

\subsection*{Solution}
Total permutations of 7 people is $7! = 5040$.
Permutations where 2 girls are together: Treat them as one block. There are 2 ways to arrange them within the block.
We now arrange 5 boys + 1 block = 6 items. Number of ways: $6! \cdot 2! = 720 \cdot 2 = 1440$.
Number of ways where girls are NOT together = Total - Together
\[
5040 - 1440 = 3600
\]
Alternatively, arrange 5 boys ($5! = 120$). This creates 6 slots (before, between, after).
_ B _ B _ B _ B _ B _
Choose 2 slots for girls: $\binom{6}{2} = 15$.
Arrange girls in those slots: $2! = 2$.
Total: $120 \cdot 15 \cdot 2 = 3600$.

\subsection*{Answer}
(B) 3600
