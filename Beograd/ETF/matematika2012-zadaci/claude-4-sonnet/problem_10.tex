\section*{Problem 10}
Ako je $a \in \mathbb{R}$ i $\left|a + \frac{1}{a}\right| = 3$, tada je $\left|a - \frac{1}{a}\right|$ jednako:

\subsection*{Solution}
Imamo $\left|a + \frac{1}{a}\right| = 3$.

Ovo znači da je ili $a + \frac{1}{a} = 3$ ili $a + \frac{1}{a} = -3$.

Koristimo identitet:
\[
\left(a + \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = a^2 + 2 + \frac{1}{a^2}
\]
\[
\left(a - \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = a^2 - 2 + \frac{1}{a^2}
\]

Oduzimanjem:
\[
\left(a + \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 - \left(a - \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = 4
\]

Pošto je $\left|a + \frac{1}{a}\right| = 3$, imamo $\left(a + \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = 9$.

Dakle:
\[
9 - \left(a - \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = 4
\]
\[
\left(a - \frac{1}{a}\right)^2 = 5
\]
\[
\left|a - \frac{1}{a}\right| = \sqrt{5}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{5}$ (opcija \textbf{A}).
