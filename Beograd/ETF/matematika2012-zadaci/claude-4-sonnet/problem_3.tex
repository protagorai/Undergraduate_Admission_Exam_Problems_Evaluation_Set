\section*{Problem 3}
Ako je $f(x) = x^3 - 3x$ i $g(x) = \sin \frac{\pi}{12} x$, tada je $f(g(2))$ jednako:

\subsection*{Solution}
Prvo računamo $g(2)$:
\[
g(2) = \sin \frac{\pi}{12} \cdot 2 = \sin \frac{\pi}{6}
\]

Znamo da je $\sin \frac{\pi}{6} = \frac{1}{2}$.

Sada računamo $f(g(2)) = f\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)$:
\[
f\left(\frac{1}{2}\right) = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^3 - 3 \cdot \frac{1}{2}
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{8} - \frac{3}{2} = \frac{1}{8} - \frac{12}{8} = \frac{1-12}{8} = -\frac{11}{8}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\frac{11}{8}$ (opcija \textbf{D}).
