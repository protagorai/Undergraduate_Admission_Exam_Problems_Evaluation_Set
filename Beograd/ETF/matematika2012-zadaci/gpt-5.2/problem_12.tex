\section*{Problem 12}
Evaluate
\[
8\sin^2 80^\circ-2\sqrt3\sin40^\circ-2\cos40^\circ.
\]
Options: (A) $2$ \quad (B) $2\sqrt3$ \quad (C) $4\sqrt3$ \quad (D) $4$ \quad (E) $1$.

\subsection*{Solution}
First,
\[
8\sin^2 80^\circ=8\cdot\frac{1-\cos160^\circ}{2}=4(1-\cos160^\circ).
\]
Since $\cos160^\circ=\cos(180^\circ-20^\circ)=-\cos20^\circ$, we get
\[
8\sin^2 80^\circ=4(1+\cos20^\circ)=4+4\cos20^\circ.
\]
Now use $\cos20^\circ=\cos(60^\circ-40^\circ)=\cos60^\circ\cos40^\circ+\sin60^\circ\sin40^\circ$:
\[
\cos20^\circ=\frac12\cos40^\circ+\frac{\sqrt3}{2}\sin40^\circ,
\]
so
\[
4\cos20^\circ=2\cos40^\circ+2\sqrt3\sin40^\circ.
\]
Therefore the whole expression equals
\[
4+\bigl(2\cos40^\circ+2\sqrt3\sin40^\circ\bigr)-2\sqrt3\sin40^\circ-2\cos40^\circ=4.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$4$ (option \textbf{D}).
