\section*{Problem 3}
If $f(x)=x^3-3x$ and $g(x)=\sin\!\left(\frac{\pi}{12}x\right)$, compute $f(g(2))$.

Options: (A) $0$ \quad (B) $-\frac{11}{2}$ \quad (C) $\frac{11}{2}$ \quad (D) $-\frac{11}{8}$ \quad (E) $\frac{11}{8}$.

\subsection*{Solution}
First,
\[
g(2)=\sin\!\left(\frac{\pi}{12}\cdot 2\right)=\sin\!\left(\frac{\pi}{6}\right)=\frac12.
\]
Then
\[
f\!\left(\frac12\right)=\left(\frac12\right)^3-3\cdot\frac12=\frac18-\frac32=\frac18-\frac{12}{8}=-\frac{11}{8}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\frac{11}{8}$ (option \textbf{D}).
