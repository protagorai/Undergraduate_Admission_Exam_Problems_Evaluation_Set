\section*{Problem 2}
Find the total number of diagonals of a regular decagon.

Options: (A) $15$ \quad (B) $20$ \quad (C) $25$ \quad (D) $30$ \quad (E) $35$.

\subsection*{Solution}
In an $n$-gon, each vertex connects by diagonals to $n-3$ other vertices (excluding itself and its two neighbors).
Thus the total number of diagonals is
\[
\frac{n(n-3)}{2}.
\]
For $n=10$:
\[
\frac{10(10-3)}{2}=\frac{10\cdot 7}{2}=35.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$35$ (option \textbf{E}).
