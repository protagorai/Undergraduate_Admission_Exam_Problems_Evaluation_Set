\section*{Problem 4}
Given complex numbers $z_1 = 2017 + 2018i$, $z_2 = 2018 + 2019i$ and 
\[
w = (z_2 - z_1)^{-2020} \cdot \left(\frac{\overline{z_1} + 1}{2018}\right)^{2021}
\]
where $\overline{z_1}$ is the complex conjugate of $z_1$ and $i^2 = -1$. Find $|w|$.

(A) $\sqrt{5}$ \quad (B) $\sqrt{2017}$ \quad (C) $\sqrt{2020}$ \quad (D) $\sqrt{2}$ \quad (E) $\sqrt{10}$

\subsection*{Solution}
First, let's compute $z_2 - z_1$:
\[
z_2 - z_1 = (2018 + 2019i) - (2017 + 2018i) = 1 + i
\]

The modulus of $z_2 - z_1$:
\[
|z_2 - z_1| = |1 + i| = \sqrt{1^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{2}
\]

Now, the complex conjugate of $z_1$:
\[
\overline{z_1} = 2017 - 2018i
\]

So:
\[
\overline{z_1} + 1 = 2018 - 2018i = 2018(1 - i)
\]

Therefore:
\[
\frac{\overline{z_1} + 1}{2018} = 1 - i
\]

The modulus:
\[
\left|\frac{\overline{z_1} + 1}{2018}\right| = |1 - i| = \sqrt{2}
\]

Now we can find $|w|$:
\[
|w| = |z_2 - z_1|^{-2020} \cdot \left|\frac{\overline{z_1} + 1}{2018}\right|^{2021}
\]
\[
|w| = (\sqrt{2})^{-2020} \cdot (\sqrt{2})^{2021} = (\sqrt{2})^{2021-2020} = (\sqrt{2})^1 = \sqrt{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(D) $\sqrt{2}$
