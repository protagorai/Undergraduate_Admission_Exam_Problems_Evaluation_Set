\section*{Problem 4}
Dati su kompleksni brojevi $z_1 = 2017 + 2018i$, $z_2 = 2018 + 2019i$ i $w = (z_2 - z_1)^{-2020} \cdot \left(\frac{z_1 + 1}{2018}\right)^{2021}$ (gde je $z_1$ konjugovano kompleksni broj broja $z_1$ i $i^2 = -1$). Tada je $|w|$ jednako:

(A) $\sqrt{5}$ \quad (B) $\sqrt{2017}$ \quad (C) $\sqrt{2020}$ \quad (D) $\sqrt{2}$ \quad (E) $\sqrt{10}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Prvo izračunajmo potrebne vrednosti:
\[
z_1 = 2017 + 2018i, \quad \overline{z_1} = 2017 - 2018i
\]
\[
z_2 = 2018 + 2019i
\]
\[
z_2 - z_1 = (2018 + 2019i) - (2017 + 2018i) = 1 + i
\]
\[
\overline{z_1} + 1 = 2017 - 2018i + 1 = 2018 - 2018i = 2018(1 - i)
\]

Dakle:
\[
w = (1 + i)^{-2020} \cdot \left(\frac{2018(1 - i)}{2018}\right)^{2021} = (1 + i)^{-2020} \cdot (1 - i)^{2021}
\]

Izračunajmo module:
\[
|1 + i| = \sqrt{1^2 + 1^2} = \sqrt{2}
\]
\[
|1 - i| = \sqrt{1^2 + (-1)^2} = \sqrt{2}
\]

Dakle:
\[
|w| = |1 + i|^{-2020} \cdot |1 - i|^{2021} = (\sqrt{2})^{-2020} \cdot (\sqrt{2})^{2021} = (\sqrt{2})^{2021-2020} = \sqrt{2}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
(D) $\sqrt{2}$
