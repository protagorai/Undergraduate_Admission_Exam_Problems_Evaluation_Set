\section*{Problem 5}
Neka je $P(x)$ polinom najmanjeg stepena ƒçiji su koeficijenti realni brojevi, a koreni $-1$ i $2i$. Ako je $P(0) = -12$, tada je $P(-2)$ jednako:
(A) $-12$
(B) 24
(C) $-30$
(D) $-36$
(E) 72
(N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Since the coefficients of $P(x)$ are real, if $2i$ is a root, then its complex conjugate $-2i$ must also be a root.
The roots are $-1$, $2i$, and $-2i$. The polynomial of the smallest degree with these roots is of the form:
\[
P(x) = A(x - (-1))(x - 2i)(x - (-2i)) = A(x+1)(x^2 + 4).
\]
We are given that $P(0) = -12$. Substituting $x=0$:
\[
P(0) = A(0+1)(0^2+4) = 4A = -12 \implies A = -3.
\]
So the polynomial is $P(x) = -3(x+1)(x^2+4)$.
We need to find $P(-2)$:
\[
P(-2) = -3(-2+1)((-2)^2+4) = -3(-1)(4+4) = 3 \cdot 8 = 24.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
24 (option \textbf{B}).
