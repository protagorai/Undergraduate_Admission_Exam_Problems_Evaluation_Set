\section*{Problem 14}
Skup svih realnih rešenja nejednačine $\frac{|3^x - 1| - |3 - 3^x| - 2}{\sqrt{4^x - 2^{x+3} + 16}} \ge 0$ je oblika (za neke realne brojeve $a, b, c$ i $d$ takve da je $-\infty < a < b < c < d < +\infty$):
(A) $(a, b)$
(B) $[a, b) \cup (b, +\infty)$
(C) $[a, +\infty)$
(D) $(a, b) \cup [c, d)$
(E) $(-\infty, a] \cup [b, c]$
(N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's analyze the denominator first.
$D = \sqrt{(2^x)^2 - 2^3 \cdot 2^x + 16} = \sqrt{(2^x)^2 - 8 \cdot 2^x + 16} = \sqrt{(2^x - 4)^2} = |2^x - 4|$.
The denominator must be non-zero, so $2^x - 4 \neq 0 \implies 2^x \neq 4 \implies x \neq 2$.
Since the denominator is always positive (where defined), the sign of the fraction depends only on the numerator.
Numerator inequality:
\[ |3^x - 1| - |3 - 3^x| - 2 \ge 0. \]
Let $t = 3^x$ where $t > 0$.
\[ |t - 1| - |3 - t| - 2 \ge 0. \]
Note $|3-t| = |t-3|$.
\[ |t - 1| - |t - 3| - 2 \ge 0. \]
Critical points are $t=1$ and $t=3$.
1. $t < 1$: $-(t-1) - (3-t) - 2 \ge 0 \implies -t+1-3+t-2 \ge 0 \implies -4 \ge 0$ (False).
2. $1 \le t \le 3$: $(t-1) - (3-t) - 2 \ge 0 \implies t-1-3+t-2 \ge 0 \implies 2t - 6 \ge 0 \implies t \ge 3$.
   Intersection with interval $[1, 3]$ is $t=3$.
3. $t > 3$: $(t-1) - (t-3) - 2 \ge 0 \implies t-1-t+3-2 \ge 0 \implies 0 \ge 0$ (True).
   This holds for all $t > 3$.

Combining results: $t \ge 3$.
Substitute back $x$: $3^x \ge 3 \implies x \ge 1$.
Taking into account the domain restriction $x \neq 2$, the solution set is:
\[ x \in [1, 2) \cup (2, +\infty). \]
This matches the form $[a, b) \cup (b, +\infty)$ with $a=1, b=2$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$[a, b) \cup (b, +\infty)$ (option \textbf{B}).
