\section*{Problem 20}
Dat je skup $A = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7\}$. Ukupan broj preslikavanja $f: A \to A$ takvih da važi
\[ (\forall k \in A) \left( f(k) \neq \min\{k, 3\} \land (k > 3 \Rightarrow f(k) < k-2) \right), \]
gde je $\min \{a, b\} = \begin{cases} a, & a \le b \\ b, & a > b \end{cases}$, jednak je:
(A) 2592
(B) 1722
(C) 3421
(D) 1628
(E) nijedan od prethodno ponuđenih odgovora
(N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
We determine the number of choices for each $k \in A$.
The condition is a conjunction of two parts.
1. $f(k) \neq \min\{k, 3\}$.
2. If $k > 3$, then $f(k) < k-2$.

Analyze for each $k$:
- $k=1$: $\min\{1, 3\} = 1$. Condition: $f(1) \neq 1$.
  Possibilities for $f(1) \in \{2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7\}$. (6 choices).
- $k=2$: $\min\{2, 3\} = 2$. Condition: $f(2) \neq 2$.
  Possibilities for $f(2) \in \{1, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7\}$. (6 choices).
- $k=3$: $\min\{3, 3\} = 3$. Condition: $f(3) \neq 3$.
  Possibilities for $f(3) \in \{1, 2, 4, 5, 6, 7\}$. (6 choices).

For $k > 3$, we have the additional condition $f(k) < k-2$, which implies $f(k) \in A$ must be small.
- $k=4$: $f(4) < 2 \implies f(4) \in \{1\}$.
  Condition 1: $\min\{4, 3\} = 3$. $f(4) \neq 3$.
  Since $1 \neq 3$, the choice $f(4)=1$ is valid. (1 choice).
- $k=5$: $f(5) < 3 \implies f(5) \in \{1, 2\}$.
  Condition 1: $\min\{5, 3\} = 3$. $f(5) \neq 3$.
  Both 1 and 2 are not 3. Valid choices: $\{1, 2\}$. (2 choices).
- $k=6$: $f(6) < 4 \implies f(6) \in \{1, 2, 3\}$.
  Condition 1: $\min\{6, 3\} = 3$. $f(6) \neq 3$.
  Valid choices: $\{1, 2\}$. (2 choices).
- $k=7$: $f(7) < 5 \implies f(7) \in \{1, 2, 3, 4\}$.
  Condition 1: $\min\{7, 3\} = 3$. $f(7) \neq 3$.
  Valid choices: $\{1, 2, 4\}$. (3 choices).

Total number of mappings is the product of independent choices:
\[ N = 6 \cdot 6 \cdot 6 \cdot 1 \cdot 2 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 = 216 \cdot 12 = 2592. \]

\subsection*{Answer}
2592 (option \textbf{A}).
