\section*{Zadatak 19}

Dati su brojevi
\[
a=\frac{\sin 1}{\sin 2},\qquad
b=\frac{\sin 2}{\sin 3},\qquad
c=\frac{\sin 3}{\sin 4}.
\]
Odrediti tačan poredak.

\subsection*{Rešenje}
U argumentima nema oznake za stepene, pa posmatramo radijane.
Znamo aproksimacije:
\[
\sin 1\approx 0.8415,\quad
\sin 2\approx 0.9093,\quad
\sin 3\approx 0.1411,\quad
\sin 4\approx -0.7568.
\]
Tada su
\[
a\approx \frac{0.8415}{0.9093}\approx 0.925>0,
\]
\[
b\approx \frac{0.9093}{0.1411}\approx 6.44>0,
\]
\[
c\approx \frac{0.1411}{-0.7568}\approx -0.186<0.
\]
Dakle važi \(c<a<b\).

\subsection*{Odgovor}
\[
\boxed{(C)\;c<a<b}
\]
