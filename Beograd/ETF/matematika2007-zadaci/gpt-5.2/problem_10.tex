\section*{Zadatak 10}

Ako su \(x_1\) i \(x_2\) rešenja jednačine \(3x^2+17x-14=0\), odrediti vrednost izraza
\[
\frac{3x_1^2+5x_1x_2+3x_2^2}{4x_1x_2^2+4x_1^2x_2}.
\]

\subsection*{Rešenje}
Po Vijetovim formulama za \(3x^2+17x-14=0\) važi
\[
s=x_1+x_2=-\frac{17}{3},\qquad p=x_1x_2=\frac{-14}{3}.
\]
Imenilac:
\[
4x_1x_2^2+4x_1^2x_2=4x_1x_2(x_1+x_2)=4ps.
\]
Brojilac:
\[
3x_1^2+5x_1x_2+3x_2^2
=3(x_1^2+x_2^2)+5p
=3\bigl((x_1+x_2)^2-2p\bigr)+5p
=3s^2-p.
\]
Računamo:
\[
3s^2-p=3\cdot\frac{289}{9}-\left(-\frac{14}{3}\right)
=\frac{289}{3}+\frac{14}{3}=\frac{303}{3}=101,
\]
\[
4ps=4\cdot\left(-\frac{14}{3}\right)\left(-\frac{17}{3}\right)
=4\cdot\frac{238}{9}=\frac{952}{9}.
\]
Zato je
\[
\frac{3x_1^2+5x_1x_2+3x_2^2}{4x_1x_2^2+4x_1^2x_2}
=\frac{101}{952/9}=\frac{909}{952}.
\]

\subsection*{Odgovor}
\[
\boxed{(A)\;\frac{909}{952}}
\]
