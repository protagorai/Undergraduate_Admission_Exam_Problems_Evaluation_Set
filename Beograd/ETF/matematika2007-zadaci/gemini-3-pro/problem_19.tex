\section*{Problem 19}
Dati su brojevi $a = \frac{\sin 1}{\sin 2}, b = \frac{\sin 2}{\sin 3}$ i $c = \frac{\sin 3}{\sin 4}$. Tada je :
\[
(A)\ a < b < c \quad (B)\ c < b < a \quad (C)\ c < a < b \quad (D)\ b < a < c \quad (E)\ a < c < b \quad (N)\ \text{Ne znam}
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Uglovi su dati u radijanima.
$1 \text{ rad} \approx 57^\circ$ (I kvadrant, $\sin > 0$)
$2 \text{ rad} \approx 115^\circ$ (II kvadrant, $\sin > 0$)
$3 \text{ rad} \approx 172^\circ$ (II kvadrant, $\sin > 0$)
$4 \text{ rad} \approx 229^\circ$ (III kvadrant, $\sin < 0$)

Analizirajmo znake:
$a = \frac{\sin 1}{\sin 2} > 0$
$b = \frac{\sin 2}{\sin 3} > 0$
$c = \frac{\sin 3}{\sin 4} < 0$ (jer je imenilac negativan)
Dakle, $c$ je najmanji broj.

Uporedimo $a$ i $b$:
$a = \frac{\sin 1}{2\sin 1 \cos 1} = \frac{1}{2\cos 1}$.
Kako je $1 \text{ rad} \approx 60^\circ$, $\cos 1 \approx 0.5$. Preciznije, $\cos 1 < \cos(\pi/4) \approx 0.7$ i $\cos 1 > 0.5$.
$a \approx \frac{1}{1.08} \approx 0.92$.
$b = \frac{\sin 2}{\sin 3}$.
$\sin 3 = \sin(\pi - 3) \approx \sin(0.14)$. Ovo je mali pozitivan broj.
$\sin 2 \approx 0.9$.
$b \approx \frac{0.9}{0.14} > 1$.
Dakle, $a < 1 < b$.

Poredak je $c < a < b$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$c < a < b$ (option \textbf{C}).
