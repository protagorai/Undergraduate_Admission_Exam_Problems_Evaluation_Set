\section*{Problem 3}
Rastojanje taƒçke $(1, -1)$ od prave $x + 2y - 4 = 0$ iznosi:

(A) $\sqrt{2}$ \quad (B) 3 \quad (C) $\sqrt{3}$ \quad (D) 4 \quad (E) $\sqrt{5}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
The distance from a point $(x_0, y_0)$ to a line $ax + by + c = 0$ is given by:
\[
d = \frac{|ax_0 + by_0 + c|}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}
\]

Here we have:
\begin{itemize}
\item Point: $(x_0, y_0) = (1, -1)$
\item Line: $x + 2y - 4 = 0$, so $a = 1$, $b = 2$, $c = -4$
\end{itemize}

Computing the distance:
\[
d = \frac{|1 \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot (-1) - 4|}{\sqrt{1^2 + 2^2}} = \frac{|1 - 2 - 4|}{\sqrt{1 + 4}} = \frac{|-5|}{\sqrt{5}} = \frac{5}{\sqrt{5}} = \sqrt{5}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{5}$ (option \textbf{E}).
