\section*{Problem 10}
Ako su $x_1$ i $x_2$ rešenja jednačine $3x^2 + 17x - 14 = 0$, tada je vrednost izraza $\displaystyle\frac{3x_1^2 + 5x_1x_2 + 3x_2^2}{4x_1x_2^2 + 4x_1^2x_2}$ jednaka:

(A) $\frac{909}{952}$ \quad (B) $\frac{303}{238}$ \quad (C) $\frac{101}{352}$ \quad (D) $\frac{5}{9}$ \quad (E) $\frac{13}{3}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
By Vieta's formulas for $3x^2 + 17x - 14 = 0$:
\[
x_1 + x_2 = -\frac{17}{3}, \quad x_1 x_2 = -\frac{14}{3}
\]

Let's simplify the expression. The numerator:
\[
3x_1^2 + 5x_1x_2 + 3x_2^2 = 3(x_1^2 + x_2^2) + 5x_1x_2 = 3[(x_1 + x_2)^2 - 2x_1x_2] + 5x_1x_2
\]
\[
= 3(x_1 + x_2)^2 - 6x_1x_2 + 5x_1x_2 = 3(x_1 + x_2)^2 - x_1x_2
\]

Substituting:
\[
= 3\left(-\frac{17}{3}\right)^2 - \left(-\frac{14}{3}\right) = 3 \cdot \frac{289}{9} + \frac{14}{3} = \frac{289}{3} + \frac{14}{3} = \frac{303}{3} = 101
\]

The denominator:
\[
4x_1x_2^2 + 4x_1^2x_2 = 4x_1x_2(x_1 + x_2) = 4 \cdot \left(-\frac{14}{3}\right) \cdot \left(-\frac{17}{3}\right) = 4 \cdot \frac{238}{9} = \frac{952}{9}
\]

The ratio:
\[
\frac{101}{\frac{952}{9}} = \frac{101 \cdot 9}{952} = \frac{909}{952}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{909}{952}$ (option \textbf{A}).
