\section*{Problem 18}
Date su funkcije: $f_1(x) = 2^{\log_2 x}$, $f_2(x) = \log_2 2^x$, $f_3(x) = x$, $f_4(x) = (x \cdot 2^{-\log_2\sqrt{x}})^2$.

Tačan je iskaz:

(A) Među funkcijama nema međusobno jednakih \quad (B) $f_1 = f_2 = f_3 \neq f_4$ \\
(C) $f_1 \neq f_2 = f_3 \neq f_4 \neq f_1$ \quad (D) $f_3 \neq f_1 = f_2 \neq f_4$ \\
(E) $f_2 = f_3 \neq f_1 = f_4$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's analyze each function:

\textbf{$f_1(x) = 2^{\log_2 x}$:}

For $x > 0$: $2^{\log_2 x} = x$

Domain: $x > 0$

\textbf{$f_2(x) = \log_2 2^x$:}

$\log_2 2^x = x$ for all real $x$

Domain: all real numbers

\textbf{$f_3(x) = x$:}

Domain: all real numbers

\textbf{$f_4(x) = (x \cdot 2^{-\log_2\sqrt{x}})^2$:}

For $x > 0$: $2^{-\log_2\sqrt{x}} = 2^{-\frac{1}{2}\log_2 x} = 2^{\log_2 x^{-1/2}} = x^{-1/2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}$

So $x \cdot 2^{-\log_2\sqrt{x}} = x \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} = \sqrt{x}$

Therefore $f_4(x) = (\sqrt{x})^2 = x$ for $x > 0$

Domain: $x > 0$

\textbf{Comparison:}
\begin{itemize}
\item $f_2$ and $f_3$ are equal on all real numbers: $f_2 = f_3$
\item $f_1$ equals $x$ only for $x > 0$, so $f_1 \neq f_2$ and $f_1 \neq f_3$ (different domains)
\item $f_4$ equals $x$ only for $x > 0$, so $f_4 \neq f_2$ and $f_4 \neq f_3$
\item $f_1$ and $f_4$ have the same domain ($x > 0$) and both equal $x$ there, so $f_1 = f_4$
\end{itemize}

Therefore: $f_2 = f_3$ and $f_1 = f_4$, but $f_1 \neq f_2$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$f_2 = f_3 \neq f_1 = f_4$ (option \textbf{E}).
