\section*{Problem 15}
U razvoju binoma $\displaystyle\left(\sqrt[4]{a^2x} + \sqrt[5]{\frac{1}{ax^2}}\right)^{13}$ $(a > 0, x > 0)$, član koji ne sadrži $x$ glasi:

(A) $1287a^3$ \quad (B) $1024a^4$ \quad (C) $390a^2$ \quad (D) $516a$ \quad (E) $52a^5$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's rewrite the terms:
\[
\sqrt[4]{a^2x} = (a^2x)^{1/4} = a^{1/2}x^{1/4}
\]
\[
\sqrt[5]{\frac{1}{ax^2}} = (ax^2)^{-1/5} = a^{-1/5}x^{-2/5}
\]

Using the binomial theorem:
\[
\left(a^{1/2}x^{1/4} + a^{-1/5}x^{-2/5}\right)^{13} = \sum_{k=0}^{13} \binom{13}{k} (a^{1/2}x^{1/4})^{13-k} (a^{-1/5}x^{-2/5})^k
\]

The general term is:
\[
\binom{13}{k} a^{(13-k)/2} x^{(13-k)/4} \cdot a^{-k/5} x^{-2k/5}
\]
\[
= \binom{13}{k} a^{(13-k)/2 - k/5} x^{(13-k)/4 - 2k/5}
\]

For the term to be independent of $x$:
\[
\frac{13-k}{4} - \frac{2k}{5} = 0
\]
\[
\frac{5(13-k) - 8k}{20} = 0
\]
\[
65 - 5k - 8k = 0
\]
\[
65 = 13k \Rightarrow k = 5
\]

With $k = 5$:
\[
\text{Power of } a = \frac{13-5}{2} - \frac{5}{5} = \frac{8}{2} - 1 = 4 - 1 = 3
\]

The coefficient:
\[
\binom{13}{5} = \frac{13!}{5! \cdot 8!} = \frac{13 \cdot 12 \cdot 11 \cdot 10 \cdot 9}{5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} = \frac{154440}{120} = 1287
\]

The term independent of $x$ is $1287a^3$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$1287a^3$ (option \textbf{A}).
