\section*{Problem 20}
Broj načina na koji se može formirati petočlana komisija od 2 matematičara i 8 fizičara, tako da u njoj bude bar jedan matematičar iznosi:

(A) 132 \quad (B) 196 \quad (C) 212 \quad (D) 314 \quad (E) 422 \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
We have 2 mathematicians and 8 physicists, and need to form a 5-member committee with at least 1 mathematician.

Total people: $2 + 8 = 10$

\textbf{Method: Total committees minus committees with no mathematicians}

Total ways to choose 5 people from 10:
\[
\binom{10}{5} = \frac{10!}{5! \cdot 5!} = 252
\]

Ways to choose 5 people with no mathematicians (all from 8 physicists):
\[
\binom{8}{5} = \frac{8!}{5! \cdot 3!} = \frac{8 \cdot 7 \cdot 6}{3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} = 56
\]

Committees with at least 1 mathematician:
\[
252 - 56 = 196
\]

\textbf{Alternative verification:}
\begin{itemize}
\item Exactly 1 mathematician: $\binom{2}{1} \cdot \binom{8}{4} = 2 \cdot 70 = 140$
\item Exactly 2 mathematicians: $\binom{2}{2} \cdot \binom{8}{3} = 1 \cdot 56 = 56$
\end{itemize}

Total: $140 + 56 = 196$ ✓

\subsection*{Answer}
$196$ (option \textbf{B}).
