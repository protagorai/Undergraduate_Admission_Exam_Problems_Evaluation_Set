\section*{Problem 7}
Lopta je upisana u kocku. Odnos povr≈°ina lopte i kocke je:

(A) $\frac{2\pi}{3}$ \quad (B) $\frac{\pi}{6}$ \quad (C) $\frac{4\pi}{3}$ \quad (D) $\frac{\pi}{12}$ \quad (E) $\frac{8\pi}{3}$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the side of the cube be $a$.

When a sphere is inscribed in a cube, the diameter of the sphere equals the side of the cube.
Thus, the radius of the sphere is $r = \frac{a}{2}$.

Surface area of the sphere:
\[
S_{\text{sphere}} = 4\pi r^2 = 4\pi \left(\frac{a}{2}\right)^2 = 4\pi \cdot \frac{a^2}{4} = \pi a^2
\]

Surface area of the cube:
\[
S_{\text{cube}} = 6a^2
\]

The ratio of the surface areas:
\[
\frac{S_{\text{sphere}}}{S_{\text{cube}}} = \frac{\pi a^2}{6a^2} = \frac{\pi}{6}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{\pi}{6}$ (option \textbf{B}).
