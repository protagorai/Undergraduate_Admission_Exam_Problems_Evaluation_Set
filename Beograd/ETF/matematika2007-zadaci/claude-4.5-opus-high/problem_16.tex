\section*{Problem 16}
U geometrijskoj progresiji je $a_1 + a_5 = 51$, $a_2 + a_6 = 102$. Za koju vrednost $n$ je zbir $n$ prvih ƒçlanova te progresije $S_n = 3069$?

(A) $n = 4$ \quad (B) $n = 6$ \quad (C) $n = 8$ \quad (D) $n = 10$ \quad (E) $n = 12$ \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
In a geometric progression, $a_n = a_1 \cdot q^{n-1}$ where $q$ is the common ratio.

From the given conditions:
\[
a_1 + a_5 = a_1 + a_1 q^4 = a_1(1 + q^4) = 51
\]
\[
a_2 + a_6 = a_1 q + a_1 q^5 = a_1 q(1 + q^4) = 102
\]

Dividing the second equation by the first:
\[
\frac{a_1 q(1 + q^4)}{a_1(1 + q^4)} = \frac{102}{51} = 2
\]
\[
q = 2
\]

From $a_1(1 + q^4) = 51$:
\[
a_1(1 + 16) = 51 \Rightarrow a_1 \cdot 17 = 51 \Rightarrow a_1 = 3
\]

The sum of the first $n$ terms:
\[
S_n = a_1 \cdot \frac{q^n - 1}{q - 1} = 3 \cdot \frac{2^n - 1}{2 - 1} = 3(2^n - 1)
\]

Setting $S_n = 3069$:
\[
3(2^n - 1) = 3069
\]
\[
2^n - 1 = 1023
\]
\[
2^n = 1024 = 2^{10}
\]
\[
n = 10
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$n = 10$ (option \textbf{D}).
