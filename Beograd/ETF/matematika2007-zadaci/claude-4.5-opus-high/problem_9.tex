\section*{Problem 9}
Neka su $\alpha$, $\beta$ i $\gamma$ uglovi a $a$, $b$ i $c$ stranice proizvoljnog trougla. Tada je 
\[
\frac{b - 2a\cos\gamma}{a\sin\gamma} + \frac{c - 2b\cos\alpha}{b\sin\alpha} + \frac{a - 2c\cos\beta}{c\sin\beta}
\]
jednako:

(A) $-2$ \quad (B) $-1$ \quad (C) $0$ \quad (D) $1$ \quad (E) Nijedan od ponuÄ‘enih odgovora \quad (N) Ne znam

\subsection*{Solution}
Using the law of cosines:
\begin{align*}
a^2 &= b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos\alpha \\
b^2 &= a^2 + c^2 - 2ac\cos\beta \\
c^2 &= a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos\gamma
\end{align*}

From the third equation: $2ab\cos\gamma = a^2 + b^2 - c^2$, so $2a\cos\gamma = \frac{a^2 + b^2 - c^2}{b}$.

Thus:
\[
b - 2a\cos\gamma = b - \frac{a^2 + b^2 - c^2}{b} = \frac{b^2 - a^2 - b^2 + c^2}{b} = \frac{c^2 - a^2}{b}
\]

Using the law of sines: $\frac{a}{\sin\alpha} = \frac{b}{\sin\beta} = \frac{c}{\sin\gamma} = 2R$

So $a\sin\gamma = a \cdot \frac{c}{2R} = \frac{ac}{2R}$.

First term:
\[
\frac{b - 2a\cos\gamma}{a\sin\gamma} = \frac{\frac{c^2 - a^2}{b}}{\frac{ac}{2R}} = \frac{(c^2 - a^2) \cdot 2R}{abc} = \frac{2R(c^2 - a^2)}{abc}
\]

Similarly:
\[
\frac{c - 2b\cos\alpha}{b\sin\alpha} = \frac{2R(a^2 - b^2)}{abc}
\]
\[
\frac{a - 2c\cos\beta}{c\sin\beta} = \frac{2R(b^2 - c^2)}{abc}
\]

Adding all three:
\[
\frac{2R}{abc}\left[(c^2 - a^2) + (a^2 - b^2) + (b^2 - c^2)\right] = \frac{2R}{abc} \cdot 0 = 0
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$0$ (option \textbf{C}).
