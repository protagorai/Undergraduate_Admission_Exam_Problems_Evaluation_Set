\section*{Problem 2}
Cena knjige se najpre poveća za 20\%, a zatim se nova cena poveća za 35\%. Ukupno povećanje cene knjige je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the original price of the book be $P$.

\textbf{First increase (20\%):}
New price $= P + 0.20P = 1.20P$

\textbf{Second increase (35\%):}
Final price $= 1.20P + 0.35 \cdot 1.20P = 1.20P \cdot 1.35 = 1.62P$

\textbf{Total percentage increase:}
\[
\frac{1.62P - P}{P} \cdot 100\% = \frac{0.62P}{P} \cdot 100\% = 62\%
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$62\%$ (option \textbf{C}).
