\section*{Problem 7}
Skup tačaka u ravni $xOy$ za koje je rastojanje do tačke $A(1,0)$ dva puta veće od rastojanja do tačke $B(-2,0)$ predstavlja:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $P(x, y)$ be a point satisfying the condition.

The distance from $P$ to $A(1,0)$ is $d_A = \sqrt{(x-1)^2 + y^2}$.

The distance from $P$ to $B(-2,0)$ is $d_B = \sqrt{(x+2)^2 + y^2}$.

The condition is $d_A = 2 d_B$:
\[
\sqrt{(x-1)^2 + y^2} = 2\sqrt{(x+2)^2 + y^2}
\]

Squaring both sides:
\[
(x-1)^2 + y^2 = 4[(x+2)^2 + y^2]
\]
\[
x^2 - 2x + 1 + y^2 = 4(x^2 + 4x + 4 + y^2)
\]
\[
x^2 - 2x + 1 + y^2 = 4x^2 + 16x + 16 + 4y^2
\]
\[
0 = 3x^2 + 18x + 15 + 3y^2
\]
\[
x^2 + 6x + 5 + y^2 = 0
\]
\[
x^2 + 6x + 9 + y^2 = 4
\]
\[
(x + 3)^2 + y^2 = 4
\]

This is the equation of a circle with center $(-3, 0)$ and radius $2$.

\subsection*{Answer}
Kružnicu (option \textbf{B}).
