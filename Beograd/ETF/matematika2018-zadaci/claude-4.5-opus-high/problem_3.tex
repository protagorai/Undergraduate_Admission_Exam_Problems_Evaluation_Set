\section*{Problem 3}
Ako za kompleksan broj $z$ va≈æi $2z + 3\bar{z} = 10 + 3i$, gde je $\bar{z}$ kompleksan broj konjugovan broju $z$, tada je moduo kompleksnog broja $z$ jednak:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $z = x + yi$ where $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$.

Then $\bar{z} = x - yi$.

Substituting into the equation:
\[
2(x + yi) + 3(x - yi) = 10 + 3i
\]
\[
2x + 2yi + 3x - 3yi = 10 + 3i
\]
\[
5x + (2y - 3y)i = 10 + 3i
\]
\[
5x - yi = 10 + 3i
\]

Comparing real and imaginary parts:
\begin{align*}
5x &= 10 \Rightarrow x = 2 \\
-y &= 3 \Rightarrow y = -3
\end{align*}

So $z = 2 - 3i$.

The modulus of $z$:
\[
|z| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = \sqrt{2^2 + (-3)^2} = \sqrt{4 + 9} = \sqrt{13}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{13}$ (option \textbf{C}).
