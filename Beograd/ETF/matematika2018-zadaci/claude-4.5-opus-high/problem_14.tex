\section*{Problem 14}
Prva dva člana rastuće geometrijske progresije su rešenja jednačine $\dfrac{2^{2\sin x}}{1 + 2^{2\sin x}} = 1 - \dfrac{3 - 2^{2\sin x}}{5 - 2^{2\sin x}}$ na intervalu $(0, \pi)$. Ako je zbir ove progresije $651\pi$, tada je ukupan broj njenih članova jednak:

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $t = 2^{2\sin x}$. Since $\sin x \in [-1, 1]$, we have $t \in [2^{-2}, 2^2] = [\frac{1}{4}, 4]$.

The equation becomes:
\[
\frac{t}{1 + t} = 1 - \frac{3 - t}{5 - t}
\]
\[
\frac{t}{1 + t} = \frac{5 - t - (3 - t)}{5 - t} = \frac{2}{5 - t}
\]

Cross-multiplying:
\[
t(5 - t) = 2(1 + t)
\]
\[
5t - t^2 = 2 + 2t
\]
\[
-t^2 + 3t - 2 = 0
\]
\[
t^2 - 3t + 2 = 0
\]
\[
(t - 1)(t - 2) = 0
\]

So $t = 1$ or $t = 2$.

\textbf{For $t = 1$:} $2^{2\sin x} = 1 = 2^0 \Rightarrow \sin x = 0$
On $(0, \pi)$: no solution (endpoints excluded).

\textbf{For $t = 2$:} $2^{2\sin x} = 2 = 2^1 \Rightarrow \sin x = \frac{1}{2}$
On $(0, \pi)$: $x = \frac{\pi}{6}$ or $x = \frac{5\pi}{6}$

The first two terms of the increasing GP are $a_1 = \frac{\pi}{6}$ and $a_2 = \frac{5\pi}{6}$.

The common ratio: $r = \frac{a_2}{a_1} = \frac{5\pi/6}{\pi/6} = 5$

Sum of GP with $n$ terms:
\[
S_n = a_1 \cdot \frac{r^n - 1}{r - 1} = \frac{\pi}{6} \cdot \frac{5^n - 1}{4} = 651\pi
\]
\[
\frac{5^n - 1}{24} = 651
\]
\[
5^n - 1 = 651 \times 24 = 15624
\]
\[
5^n = 15625 = 5^6
\]
\[
n = 6
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$6$ (option \textbf{B}).
