\section*{Problem 10}
Vrednost realnog parametra $\alpha$ za koju sistem jednačina $2x - 5y = 0$, $3x - \alpha y = 0$ ima više od jednog rešenja je:

\subsection*{Solution}
This is a homogeneous system of linear equations. Such a system always has the trivial solution $(0, 0)$.

For the system to have more than one solution (infinitely many solutions), the two equations must be linearly dependent (proportional).

The system in matrix form:
\[
\begin{pmatrix} 2 & -5 \\ 3 & -\alpha \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}
\]

For infinitely many solutions, the determinant must equal zero:
\[
\det \begin{pmatrix} 2 & -5 \\ 3 & -\alpha \end{pmatrix} = 0
\]
\[
2(-\alpha) - (-5)(3) = 0
\]
\[
-2\alpha + 15 = 0
\]
\[
\alpha = \frac{15}{2}
\]

Let's verify: When $\alpha = \frac{15}{2}$, the second equation becomes $3x - \frac{15}{2}y = 0$, or $6x - 15y = 0$, which is $3(2x - 5y) = 0$. This is indeed proportional to the first equation.

\subsection*{Answer}
$\dfrac{15}{2}$ (option \textbf{E}).
