\section*{Problem 6}
Ako je $\tan\alpha=-7$, $\alpha\in(\pi/2,\pi)$, tada
\[
\frac{3\sin\alpha+\cos\alpha}{\cos\alpha-3\sin\alpha}
\]
iznosi:
\[
\text{(A) }\frac{10}{11}\quad \text{(B) }-\frac{10}{11}\quad \text{(C) }\frac{11}{20}\quad \text{(D) }-\frac{11}{10}\quad \text{(E) }\frac{11}{10}\quad \text{(N) Ne znam.}
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Podelimo brojilac i imenilac sa $\cos\alpha\neq 0$:
\[
\frac{3\sin\alpha+\cos\alpha}{\cos\alpha-3\sin\alpha}
\;=\;
\frac{3\tan\alpha+1}{1-3\tan\alpha}.
\]
Za $\tan\alpha=-7$ dobijamo
\[
\frac{3(-7)+1}{1-3(-7)}=\frac{-21+1}{1+21}=\frac{-20}{22}=-\frac{10}{11}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-\dfrac{10}{11}$ (option \textbf{B}).
