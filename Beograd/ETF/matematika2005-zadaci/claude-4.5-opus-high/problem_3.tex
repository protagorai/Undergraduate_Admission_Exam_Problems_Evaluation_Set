\section*{Problem 3}
If $x$ is $x\%$ of $y$, where $x$ is a positive real number, find the value of $y$.

\subsection*{Solution}
The statement "$x$ is $x\%$ of $y$" can be written mathematically as:
\[
x = \frac{x}{100} \cdot y
\]

Solving for $y$:
\[
x = \frac{xy}{100}
\]

Since $x > 0$, we can divide both sides by $x$:
\[
1 = \frac{y}{100}
\]

Therefore:
\[
y = 100
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$100$ (option \textbf{A}).
