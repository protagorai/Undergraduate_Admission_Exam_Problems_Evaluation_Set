\section*{Problem 7}
In a geometric progression with common ratio 2, the sum of the first seven terms is 635. Find the seventh term.

\subsection*{Solution}
Let the first term be $a_1$ and common ratio $r = 2$.

The sum of first $n$ terms of a geometric progression:
\[
S_n = a_1 \cdot \frac{r^n - 1}{r - 1}
\]

For $n = 7$ and $r = 2$:
\[
S_7 = a_1 \cdot \frac{2^7 - 1}{2 - 1} = a_1 \cdot \frac{128 - 1}{1} = 127a_1
\]

Given $S_7 = 635$:
\[
127a_1 = 635 \implies a_1 = \frac{635}{127} = 5
\]

The seventh term:
\[
a_7 = a_1 \cdot r^6 = 5 \cdot 2^6 = 5 \cdot 64 = 320
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$320$ (option \textbf{E}).
