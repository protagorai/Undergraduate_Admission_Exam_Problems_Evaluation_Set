\section*{Problem 7}
Prvi izvod funkcije $f(x) = \ln \frac{x^2-1+\sqrt{x^4+1}}{x}$ u taƒçki $x_0=1$ jednak je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Rewrite $f(x)$:
\[ f(x) = \ln(x^2 - 1 + \sqrt{x^4+1}) - \ln x \]
Differentiate:
\[ f'(x) = \frac{1}{x^2 - 1 + \sqrt{x^4+1}} \left( 2x + \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x^4+1}} \cdot 4x^3 \right) - \frac{1}{x} \]
Evaluate at $x=1$:
Term inside log: $1^2 - 1 + \sqrt{1+1} = \sqrt{2}$.
Derivative part 1: $\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} (2 + \frac{4}{2\sqrt{2}}) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} (2 + \sqrt{2}) = \frac{2}{\sqrt{2}} + 1 = \sqrt{2} + 1$.
Derivative part 2: $-1/1 = -1$.
Total: $f'(1) = (\sqrt{2} + 1) - 1 = \sqrt{2}$.


\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{2}$ (option \textbf{D})
