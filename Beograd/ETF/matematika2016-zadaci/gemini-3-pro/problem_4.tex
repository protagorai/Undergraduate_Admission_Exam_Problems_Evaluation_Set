\section*{Problem 4}
Tetive kruga su $AB$ i $CD$, međusobno su normalne i seku se u tački $M$ tako da je $AM=3\text{ cm}$, $MB=4\text{ cm}$, $CM=2\text{ cm}$ i $MD=6\text{ cm}$. Prečnik tog kruga je jednak (u cm):

\subsection*{Solution}
Set up a coordinate system with $M$ at the origin $(0,0)$.
Since chords are perpendicular, let $AB$ lie on the x-axis and $CD$ on the y-axis.
Coordinates of points:
$A(-3, 0)$, $B(4, 0)$, $C(0, 2)$, $D(0, -6)$.
The center of the circle $(p, q)$ lies on the perpendicular bisectors of the chords.
For chord $AB$ (on x-axis), the bisector is $x = \frac{-3+4}{2} = 0.5$. So $p = 0.5$.
For chord $CD$ (on y-axis), the bisector is $y = \frac{2+(-6)}{2} = -2$. So $q = -2$.
Center is $O(0.5, -2)$.
Radius $R$ is the distance from $O$ to any point on the circle, e.g., $B(4,0)$.
\[ R^2 = (4 - 0.5)^2 + (0 - (-2))^2 = 3.5^2 + 2^2 = 12.25 + 4 = 16.25 = \frac{65}{4} \]
Diameter $D = 2R = 2\sqrt{\frac{65}{4}} = 2 \frac{\sqrt{65}}{2} = \sqrt{65}$.


\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{65}$ (option \textbf{C})
