\section*{Problem 3}
Dat je kompleksan broj $z = \frac{\sqrt{2016}+i^{2019}}{\sqrt{2016}+i^{2017}}$, $(i^2=-1)$. Tada je izraz $\frac{z+\bar{z}}{2}$ (gde je $\bar{z}$ konjugovano kompleksni broj broja $z$) jednak:

\subsection*{Solution}
Simplify powers of $i$:
\[ i^{2019} = i^{4 \cdot 504 + 3} = i^3 = -i \]
\[ i^{2017} = i^{4 \cdot 504 + 1} = i^1 = i \]
Substitute into $z$:
\[ z = \frac{\sqrt{2016}-i}{\sqrt{2016}+i} \]
To remove the complex denominator, multiply numerator and denominator by the conjugate $\sqrt{2016}-i$:
\[ z = \frac{(\sqrt{2016}-i)^2}{(\sqrt{2016}+i)(\sqrt{2016}-i)} = \frac{2016 - 2i\sqrt{2016} + i^2}{2016 - i^2} \]
\[ z = \frac{2016 - 1 - 2i\sqrt{2016}}{2016 + 1} = \frac{2015 - 2i\sqrt{2016}}{2017} \]
\[ z = \frac{2015}{2017} - i \frac{2\sqrt{2016}}{2017} \]
The expression $\frac{z+\bar{z}}{2}$ represents the real part of $z$, Re($z$).
\[ \text{Re}(z) = \frac{2015}{2017} \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{2015}{2017}$ (option \textbf{C})
