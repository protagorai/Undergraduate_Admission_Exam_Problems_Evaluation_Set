\section*{Problem 18}
Zbir svih realnih rešenja jednačine $2\sqrt{x} \cdot 4^x + 5 \cdot 2^{x+1} + 2\sqrt{x} = 2^{2x+2} + 5\sqrt{x} \cdot 2^x + 4$ je:

\subsection*{Solution}
Rearrange terms:
$2\sqrt{x} \cdot 2^{2x} - 4 \cdot 2^{2x} - 5\sqrt{x} \cdot 2^x + 10 \cdot 2^x + 2\sqrt{x} - 4 = 0$.
$2^{2x}(2\sqrt{x}-4) - 5 \cdot 2^x(\sqrt{x}-2) + 2(\sqrt{x}-2) = 0$.
$2(\sqrt{x}-2) 2^{2x} - 5(\sqrt{x}-2) 2^x + 2(\sqrt{x}-2) = 0$.
$(\sqrt{x}-2) (2(2^x)^2 - 5(2^x) + 2) = 0$.
1) $\sqrt{x}=2 \implies x=4$.
2) $2t^2 - 5t + 2 = 0$ with $t=2^x$.
$(2t-1)(t-2)=0 \implies t=1/2, t=2$.
$2^x = 2^{-1} \implies x=-1$ (invalid as $\sqrt{x}$ exists).
$2^x = 2^1 \implies x=1$.
Sum: $4+1=5$.


\subsection*{Answer}
5 (option \textbf{A})
