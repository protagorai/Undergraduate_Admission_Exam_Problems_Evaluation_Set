\section*{Problem 1}
Vrednost izraza $0,5^{1,5} \cdot 0,25^{0,5} \cdot 8^{-1,5}$ jednaka je:

\subsection*{Solution}
We convert the bases to powers of 2:
\[ 0,5 = \frac{1}{2} = 2^{-1} \]
\[ 0,25 = \frac{1}{4} = 2^{-2} \]
\[ 8 = 2^3 \]
Substitute these into the expression:
\[ (2^{-1})^{1,5} \cdot (2^{-2})^{0,5} \cdot (2^3)^{-1,5} \]
Using the property $(a^m)^n = a^{mn}$:
\[ 2^{-1,5} \cdot 2^{-1} \cdot 2^{-4,5} \]
Using $a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}$:
\[ 2^{-1,5 - 1 - 4,5} = 2^{-7} = \frac{1}{2^7} \]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\frac{1}{2^7}$ (option \textbf{C})
