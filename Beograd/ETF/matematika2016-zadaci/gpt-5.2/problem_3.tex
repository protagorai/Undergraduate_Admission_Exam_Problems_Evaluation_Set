\section*{Problem 3}
Let
\[
z=\frac{\sqrt{2016}+i^{2019}}{\sqrt{2016}+i^{2017}},\qquad (i^2=-1).
\]
Compute
\[
\frac{z+\overline z}{2}.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Since $i^4=1$, we have
\[
i^{2019}=i^{4\cdot 504+3}=i^3=-i,\qquad i^{2017}=i^{4\cdot 504+1}=i.
\]
Let $a=\sqrt{2016}$. Then
\[
z=\frac{a-i}{a+i}.
\]
Multiply numerator and denominator by the conjugate $(a-i)$:
\[
z=\frac{(a-i)^2}{a^2+1}=\frac{a^2-2ai-1}{a^2+1}.
\]
Hence the real part is
\[
\Re(z)=\frac{a^2-1}{a^2+1}=\frac{2016-1}{2016+1}=\frac{2015}{2017}.
\]
But $\dfrac{z+\overline z}{2}=\Re(z)$, so the value is $\dfrac{2015}{2017}$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$\dfrac{2015}{2017}$ (option \textbf{C}).
