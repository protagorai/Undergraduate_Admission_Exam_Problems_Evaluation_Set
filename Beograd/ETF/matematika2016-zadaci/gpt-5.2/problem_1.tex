\section*{Problem 1}
Compute
\[
0.5^{1.5}\cdot 0.25^{0.5}\cdot 8^{-1.5}.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Rewrite everything as powers of $2$:
\[
0.5=\frac12=2^{-1},\qquad 0.25=\frac14=2^{-2},\qquad 8=2^3.
\]
Hence
\[
0.5^{1.5}=(2^{-1})^{3/2}=2^{-3/2},\quad
0.25^{0.5}=(2^{-2})^{1/2}=2^{-1},\quad
8^{-1.5}=(2^3)^{-3/2}=2^{-9/2}.
\]
Multiplying:
\[
2^{-3/2}\cdot 2^{-1}\cdot 2^{-9/2}
=2^{-(3/2+1+9/2)}=2^{-7}=\frac{1}{2^7}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\dfrac{1}{2^7}$ (option \textbf{C}).
