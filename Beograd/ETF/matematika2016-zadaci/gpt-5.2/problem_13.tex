\section*{Problem 13}
If the polynomial
\[
P(x)=x^{2016}+x^{2015}-x^{2014}+ax^{2013}-bx^2+c \qquad (a,b,c\in\mathbb{R})
\]
is divisible by $x^3-x$, find $4a^2+3b^2+8c^2$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Since $x^3-x=x(x-1)(x+1)$, divisibility implies
\[
P(0)=P(1)=P(-1)=0.
\]
First,
\[
P(0)=c=0\Rightarrow c=0.
\]
Next,
\[
P(1)=1+1-1+a-b+c=1+a-b=0\Rightarrow b=1+a.
\]
Finally, using parity of powers at $x=-1$:
\[
P(-1)=1-1-1-a-b+c=-1-a-b=0\Rightarrow b=-1-a.
\]
Thus $1+a=-1-a\Rightarrow a=-1$, hence $b=0$ and $c=0$.
Therefore
\[
4a^2+3b^2+8c^2=4\cdot 1+0+0=4.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$4$ (option \textbf{A}).
