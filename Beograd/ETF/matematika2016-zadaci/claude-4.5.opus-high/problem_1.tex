\section*{Problem 1}
Find the value of the expression $0.5^{1.5} \cdot 0.25^{0.5} \cdot 8^{-1.5}$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Let's convert all terms to powers of 2:
\begin{align*}
0.5 &= \frac{1}{2} = 2^{-1}\\
0.25 &= \frac{1}{4} = 2^{-2}\\
8 &= 2^3
\end{align*}

Now we can rewrite the expression:
\begin{align*}
0.5^{1.5} \cdot 0.25^{0.5} \cdot 8^{-1.5} &= (2^{-1})^{1.5} \cdot (2^{-2})^{0.5} \cdot (2^3)^{-1.5}\\
&= 2^{-1.5} \cdot 2^{-1} \cdot 2^{-4.5}\\
&= 2^{-1.5-1-4.5}\\
&= 2^{-7}\\
&= \frac{1}{2^7}
\end{align*}

\subsection*{Answer}
$\dfrac{1}{2^7}$ (option \textbf{C}).
