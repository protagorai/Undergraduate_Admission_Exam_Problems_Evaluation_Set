\section*{Problem 5}
Find the solution to the equation $\sqrt{x}\log \sqrt{x} = 10$:

A) $2$; B) $1$; C) $\frac{1}{100}$; D) $100$; E) $10$; N) Ne znam.

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $y = \sqrt{x}$, so $x = y^2$ and $y > 0$.

The equation becomes:
\[
y \log y = 10
\]

We need to solve for $y$. Let's try the given options by substituting back to find $x$:

If $x = 100$, then $y = \sqrt{100} = 10$.
Check: $10 \log 10 = 10 \cdot 1 = 10$ ✓

Let's verify this is correct. We have $\sqrt{x} = 10$, so $x = 100$.
Then $\sqrt{x} \log \sqrt{x} = 10 \log 10 = 10 \cdot 1 = 10$ ✓

\subsection*{Answer}
$100$ (option \textbf{D}).
