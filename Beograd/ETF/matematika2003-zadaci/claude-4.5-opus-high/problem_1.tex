\section*{Problem 1}
Ako je $a = \frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{2}$ i $b = \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{2}$, tada je $a^2 - b^2$ jednako:

\textbf{A)} $\sqrt{5}$; \quad \textbf{B)} 10; \quad \textbf{C)} $5\sqrt{5}$; \quad \textbf{D)} 5; \quad \textbf{E)} $\frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}$

\subsection*{Solution}
Koristimo formulu za razliku kvadrata:
\[
a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)
\]

Izračunajmo $a + b$:
\[
a + b = \frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{2} + \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{5}+1+\sqrt{5}-1}{2} = \frac{2\sqrt{5}}{2} = \sqrt{5}
\]

Izračunajmo $a - b$:
\[
a - b = \frac{\sqrt{5}+1}{2} - \frac{\sqrt{5}-1}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{5}+1-\sqrt{5}+1}{2} = \frac{2}{2} = 1
\]

Dakle:
\[
a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b) = \sqrt{5} \cdot 1 = \sqrt{5}
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt{5}$ (option \textbf{A}).
