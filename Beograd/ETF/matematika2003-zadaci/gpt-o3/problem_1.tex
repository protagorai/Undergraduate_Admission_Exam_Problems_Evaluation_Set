\section*{Problem 1}
Ako je
\[
 a=\frac{\sqrt5+1}{2}, \qquad b=\frac{\sqrt5-1}{2},
\]
odredi $a^2-b^2$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Izraƒçunajmo kvadrate brojeva $a$ i $b$:
\[
\begin{aligned}
a^2&=\left(\frac{\sqrt5+1}{2}\right)^2=\frac{(\sqrt5+1)^2}{4}
      =\frac{5+2\sqrt5+1}{4}=\frac{6+2\sqrt5}{4}=\frac{3+\sqrt5}{2},\\[6pt]
b^2&=\left(\frac{\sqrt5-1}{2}\right)^2=\frac{(\sqrt5-1)^2}{4}
      =\frac{5-2\sqrt5+1}{4}=\frac{6-2\sqrt5}{4}=\frac{3-\sqrt5}{2}.
\end{aligned}
\]
Razlika je
\[
 a^2-b^2=\frac{3+\sqrt5}{2}-\frac{3-\sqrt5}{2}=\frac{2\sqrt5}{2}=\boxed{\sqrt5}.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$\sqrt5$ (opcija \textbf{A}).
