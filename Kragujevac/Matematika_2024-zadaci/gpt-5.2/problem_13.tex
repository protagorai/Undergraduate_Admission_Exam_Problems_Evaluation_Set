\section*{Problem 13}
Let
\[
z=\left(\frac{2-3i}{1+i}+\frac{2+i}{1-i}\right)^{2024},\qquad i^2=-1.
\]
Compute $z^{2024}$.

\subsection*{Solution}
Simplify each fraction by multiplying by the conjugate.
\[
\frac{2-3i}{1+i}=\frac{(2-3i)(1-i)}{(1+i)(1-i)}
=\frac{2-2i-3i+3i^2}{2}
=\frac{-1-5i}{2}.
\]
\[
\frac{2+i}{1-i}=\frac{(2+i)(1+i)}{(1-i)(1+i)}
=\frac{2+2i+i+i^2}{2}
=\frac{1+3i}{2}.
\]
Sum:
\[
\frac{-1-5i}{2}+\frac{1+3i}{2}=\frac{-2i}{2}=-i.
\]
So
\[
z=(-i)^{2024}.
\]
Since $(-i)^4=1$ and $2024\equiv 0\pmod 4$, we get $(-i)^{2024}=1$, hence $z=1$ and therefore
\[
z^{2024}=1^{2024}=1.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$1$ (option \textbf{Ð’}).
