\section*{Problem 6}
Find the number of integer solutions of
\[
3\cdot 16^x + 2\cdot 81^x \le 5\cdot 36^x.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Rewrite:
\[
16^x=2^{4x},\quad 81^x=3^{4x},\quad 36^x=(2^2\cdot 3^2)^x=2^{2x}3^{2x}.
\]
Let $u=2^{2x}>0$ and $v=3^{2x}>0$. Then $2^{4x}=u^2$, $3^{4x}=v^2$, and $36^x=uv$.
The inequality becomes
\[
3u^2+2v^2\le 5uv.
\]
Divide by $v^2$ and set $t=\frac{u}{v}=\left(\frac{4}{9}\right)^x>0$:
\[
3t^2+2\le 5t \;\Rightarrow\; 3t^2-5t+2\le 0
\;\Rightarrow\; (3t-2)(t-1)\le 0.
\]
Hence
\[
\frac{2}{3}\le t\le 1.
\]
Since $\left(\frac49\right)^x$ is decreasing, this corresponds to $x\in[0,\tfrac12]$.
Thus the only integer solution is $x=0$.

\subsection*{Answer}
$1$ integer solution (option \textbf{A}).
