\section*{Problem 2 - Solution}

\textbf{Problem:} Find the sum of integer solutions of the inequality $3|x-1| + x \leq 4$.

\textbf{Solution:}

We need to consider two cases based on the absolute value.

\textbf{Case 1:} $x \geq 1$ (then $|x-1| = x-1$)
\begin{align*}
3(x-1) + x &\leq 4 \\
3x - 3 + x &\leq 4 \\
4x &\leq 7 \\
x &\leq \frac{7}{4} = 1.75
\end{align*}
Combined with $x \geq 1$: $1 \leq x \leq 1.75$

Integer solution in this interval: $x = 1$

\textbf{Case 2:} $x < 1$ (then $|x-1| = 1-x$)
\begin{align*}
3(1-x) + x &\leq 4 \\
3 - 3x + x &\leq 4 \\
3 - 2x &\leq 4 \\
-2x &\leq 1 \\
x &\geq -\frac{1}{2}
\end{align*}
Combined with $x < 1$: $-0.5 \leq x < 1$

Integer solution in this interval: $x = 0$

\textbf{Sum of all integer solutions:} $0 + 1 = 1$
