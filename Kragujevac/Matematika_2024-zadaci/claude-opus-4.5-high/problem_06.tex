\section*{Problem 6 - Solution}

\textbf{Problem:} Find the number of integer solutions of the inequality $3 \cdot 16^x + 2 \cdot 81^x \leq 5 \cdot 36^x$.

\textbf{Solution:}

Divide both sides by $36^x$ (which is always positive):
\[
3 \cdot \frac{16^x}{36^x} + 2 \cdot \frac{81^x}{36^x} \leq 5
\]

\[
3 \cdot \left(\frac{16}{36}\right)^x + 2 \cdot \left(\frac{81}{36}\right)^x \leq 5
\]

\[
3 \cdot \left(\frac{4}{6}\right)^{2x} + 2 \cdot \left(\frac{9}{6}\right)^{2x} \leq 5
\]

\[
3 \cdot \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2x} + 2 \cdot \left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{2x} \leq 5
\]

Let $t = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2x}$, where $t > 0$. Then $\left(\frac{3}{2}\right)^{2x} = \frac{1}{t}$.

\[
3t + \frac{2}{t} \leq 5
\]

Multiply by $t > 0$:
\[
3t^2 + 2 \leq 5t
\]
\[
3t^2 - 5t + 2 \leq 0
\]
\[
(3t - 2)(t - 1) \leq 0
\]

Solution: $\frac{2}{3} \leq t \leq 1$

Now convert back to $x$:
\begin{itemize}
\item $t \geq \frac{2}{3}$: $\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2x} \geq \frac{2}{3} = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^1$ $\implies 2x \leq 1 \implies x \leq \frac{1}{2}$
\item $t \leq 1$: $\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^{2x} \leq 1 = \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^0$ $\implies 2x \geq 0 \implies x \geq 0$
\end{itemize}

Therefore: $0 \leq x \leq \frac{1}{2}$

Integer solutions: only $x = 0$

\textbf{Number of integer solutions: 1}

\textbf{Answer: –ê) 1}
