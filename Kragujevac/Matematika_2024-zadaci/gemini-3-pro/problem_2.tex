\section*{Problem 2}
Find the sum of all integer solutions of
\[
3|x-1|+x\le 4.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $t=x-1$, so $x=t+1$. Then
\[
3|t|+(t+1)\le 4 \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad 3|t|+t\le 3.
\]
If $t\ge 0$, then $3t+t\le 3\Rightarrow 4t\le 3\Rightarrow t=0$, giving $x=1$.

If $t<0$, then $3(-t)+t\le 3\Rightarrow -2t\le 3\Rightarrow t\ge -\frac32$, hence $t=-1$, giving $x=0$.

Thus the integer solutions are $x\in\{0,1\}$ and their sum is $0+1=1$.
