\section*{Problem 5}
Find the sum of all real solutions of
\[
(x+3)\sqrt{12-|x|}=0.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
The square root requires $12-|x|\ge 0$, i.e. $|x|\le 12$.
The product equals $0$ if either factor is $0$:
\[
x+3=0 \Rightarrow x=-3,
\]
or
\[
\sqrt{12-|x|}=0 \Rightarrow 12-|x|=0 \Rightarrow |x|=12 \Rightarrow x=\pm 12.
\]
Sum of all solutions:
\[
(-3)+12+(-12)=-3.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$-3$ (option \textbf{Ð‘}).
