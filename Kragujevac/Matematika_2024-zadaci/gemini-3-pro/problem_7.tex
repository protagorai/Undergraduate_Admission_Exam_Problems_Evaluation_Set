\section*{Problem 7}
Find the sum of squares of all solutions of
\[
\frac{8\log_{16}x}{1+2\log_{16}x}=\frac{\log_{16}x}{1-\log_{16}x}+1.
\]

\subsection*{Solution}
Let $t=\log_{16}x$. Domain: $x>0$, and $1+2t\ne 0$, $1-t\ne 0$ (so $t\ne -\tfrac12,1$).
Then
\[
\frac{8t}{1+2t}=\frac{t}{1-t}+1=\frac{t+(1-t)}{1-t}=\frac{1}{1-t}.
\]
Cross-multiply:
\[
8t(1-t)=1+2t \Rightarrow -8t^2+6t-1=0 \Rightarrow 8t^2-6t+1=0.
\]
Solve:
\[
t=\frac{6\pm 2}{16}\in\left\{\frac12,\frac14\right\}.
\]
Thus $x=16^t\in\{16^{1/2},16^{1/4}\}=\{4,2\}$.
Sum of squares:
\[
4^2+2^2=16+4=20.
\]

\subsection*{Answer}
$20$ (option \textbf{Ð’}).
